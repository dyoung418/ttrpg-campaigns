The GM is considering making [[The Wriggling Man]] and the demon that killed Riven's parents (Aelir and Lytha, Kyonin ambush — see the open plot hook `campaigns/kingmaker/plot-hooks/The Demon's Legacy.md`) **one and the same entity**. Brainstormed 2026-07-02 via /ideate; **not yet canon** — no campaign note asserts the merge yet.

**Canvas:** all idea seeds are mapped in `campaigns/kingmaker/The Demon and the Worm Ideas.canvas` (the GM's first canvas; he rearranged it himself, so preserve his layout when editing). The five seeds, in short:

1. **They killed him first** — the Wriggling Man's "failed mission to the First World" failed *because* Aelir and Lytha stopped him in Kyonin; his mortal death and their "demon ambush" are the same event. His parents are the reason he is what he is.
2. **A boy's word for a thing with no name** — he's an aberration, not a demon; "demon" is what child-Riven called the horror. Every demon Riven has hunted since was the wrong one.
3. **Feywind gets recognized first** — in the K17 throne-room reveal, his angel disguise cracks at the sight of Aelir's bow ("Where did you get that bow?"), the last thing his mortal eyes saw. Recognition beat runs monster→Riven.
4. **Dream-mining goes back further** — Nyrissa's window into Riven's dreams (established Session 100) opened at the ambush; with the Horned Hunter dead (Session 100), the Wriggling Man is her oldest remaining link to the party.
5. **The symmetry** — the same entity took his parents and took Lyriel, both times while Riven was elsewhere; K17 is the first door he reaches *on time*.

**Open question the GM should answer before canonizing:** does the Wriggling Man *remember* killing them (a confrontation Riven can win) or not (the horror of the footnote)?

**Why:** the K17 throne-room encounter is imminent (Session 103 is planned; the Wriggling Man recognition beat is parked for the session after the Rimebridge), so this decision has a real deadline.

**How to apply:** if the GM adopts the merge, canonize across `npcs/The Wriggling Man.md`, `npcs/Aelir and Lytha.md`, `plot-hooks/The Demon's Legacy.md`, `characters/Riven.md`, and `npcs/Nyrissa.md`, and fold the chosen beats into the next session plan. If he rejects it, delete this memory and (ask first) the canvas.
