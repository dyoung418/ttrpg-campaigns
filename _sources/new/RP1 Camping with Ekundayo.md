Riven, Astor and Pavel are on a weeklong scouting trip with Ekundayo to explore the northern reaches of the Greenbelt, and hopefully do some bonding with the enigmatic ranger.
Pavel tries to figure out what would interest or impress Ekundayo, but humanoids remain as much a mystery to him as ever.  Frustrated, he turns his attention to Ekundayo's dog, Dog.  While scratching the dog's ears, he unexpectedly starts opening up to those who are nearby.  He mentions working in a kennel from a young age, after his mother died, and sleeping in a room next to the kennel.  He reminisces fondly about those dogs.

After five days in the wilderness together, Riven had started to open up to Pavel, Astor and Ekundayo. He shares a deeply held secret and major failure from his own past that he left his life of scouting (he was captain in the elven army) because he once was responsible for a patrol that missed an approaching army that massacred several elven villages. The scouting patrol he led missed it because they had tarried too long in a nearby village at his leadership instead of being in the field. When the enemy army began their attack, his patrol returned and he fought valiantly but was disgraced and left. He feels deep shame to this day even though it is much later. He doesn't share why he tarried in the village.
