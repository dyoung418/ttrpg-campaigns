* Session 1
	* **Feast at Jamandi Aldori's estate** in the city of Restov where she extends invitations to our PCs and other groups to go into the Stolen Lands south of Restov and create new kingdoms allied with Restov
		* Other groups also at this feast include the following NPCs
			* [[Maegar Varn]]
			* [[Harrim]]
			* [[Linzi]]
			* [[Tartuccio]]
			* [[Valerie]]
			* [[Amiri]]
			* [[Hannis Drevlev]]
			* [[The Iron Wraiths]]
	* The estate is attacked at night by a group called the The Black Tears.  The PCs are instrumental in defeating them, thus proving their worth to Jamandi Aldori
	* The PCs are given a charter to establish a kingdom in the area of the Stolen Lands known as the The Rostland Hinterlands and Greenbelt
* Session 5
	* The PCs travel to their designated area and their first stop is **[[Oleg's Trading Post]]**, a small post run by [[Oleg Leveton]] and his wife [[Svetlana Leveton]].  When they speak to Oleg and Svetlana, they find out that a group of bandits has been coming by and terrorizing them.
	* They meet [[Ekundayo]] here at Oleg's trading post.  He wants to help them
		* [[RP1 Camping with Ekundayo]]  takes place here
		* [[RP2 After the Thylacine Fight]] also takes place here
	* the PCs determine that they will defeat the bandit group terrorizing Oleg and Svetlana.  they track them to their [[Thorn River Camp]] and battle them.  In that battle, they meet [[Cassandra]], who is one of the bandits, but clearly wants to turn a new leaf and break from the bandit leader who is called  [[The Stag Lord]]
* Session 6
	* The PCs come to **[[The Stag Lord's Fort]]** and create a plan to overthrow him.  Some of them infiltrate the camp dressed as new bandit recruits, and others attack from without the camp.
	* After a long, difficult battle, the PC kill the stag lord and his father who is imprissoned by the stag lord in an underground bunker.
	* The PCs decide to make [[The Stag Lord's Fort]] their base of operations and the center of their new kingdom.  They call the small town that they establish there [[Nightmarch]] and they name their new kingdom [[Freehaven]]
		* [[RP3 After the Stag Lord Fight]] takes place here.
* Sessions 7-19
	* In the early days of Freehaven, the PCs explore the regions around Nightmarch, mapping terrain, having encounters, clearing dangers and expanding their kingdom.  Here are some of the places and encounters that they overcome in the next several sessions
		* Session 7: [[Fort Serenko]] and old fort north of Nightmarch.  No dangers encountered and the existing fort makes a good location for a new town.
		* Session 8:  [[The Temple of the Elk]] an old shrine protected by a cursed grizzly bear.
		* Session 9: Nettles Crossing, a river crossing haunted by the undead ghost of Nettle, who was murdered by the Stag Lord's bandits
		* Session 10: **The Sootscale Caverns** and the **Old Sycamore Tree**: a cave system occupied by a group of kobolds called the Sootscales.  They were currently at war with neighboring community of Mitflits in the Old Sycamore Tree.
			* It turns out the kobold group had their leader killed and replaced with [[Tartuccio]] who disguised himself as their old leader.  The PCs killed tartuccio and restored the kobolds, which helped with the peace with the mitflits.
		* Session 12: The Lonely Barrow.  A Barrow wight haunted by undead.  The PCs clear it out
		* Session 13: **[[Lizardfolk Village]]**  A village set on an island in the middle of the [[Tuskwater River]] (which also borders Nightmarch)  Lizardfolk Village is north of another island called [[Candlemere Island]] which is visibly haunted by will'o'wisps.  The Lizardfolk Village is led by [[King Vesket]].  During this encounter, the lizardfolk initially attack the PCs, but when the PCs save King Vesket from a will'o wisp, he creates a truce with the PCs.  This village eventually joins the kingdom of Freehaven and become the city of [[Vesket]]
		* Session 18: **[[Candlemere Island]]**  The PCs clear the island of its will'o wisp pests and find an undergrown compound that goes for 2 levels.  They defeat creatures in this compound, but find a mysterious sealed passage at the end of the second level which emits fear and evil.   They don't open the seal at this time, but set a guard around it.
			* The group meets [[Octavia]].
* Session 20-25
	* During this period, several events happen in the kingdom of Freehaven which challenges the PCs in their new role as leaders of the kingdom
		* Session 20: A **werewolf** (a human named Kundal) starts stalking Nightmarch, killing victims.  The PCs are able to track him down and end his reign of terror
		* Session 21: **[[Grigori]]**, a devilishly cunning propogandist comes into town and starts spreading rumors and drumming up discontent with the PCs leadership.  He twists every action of the PCs into a conspiracy and evidence of the PCs greed and contempt for the kingdom's occupants.  Grigori is remarkably effective and the kingdom starts becoming discontent.  Unknown at this point is that the Kingdom of Pitax is behind sending Grigori to cause trouble in the new kingdom.  The PCs put him on trial and issue an order to exile him, but also see to it that he doesn't make it very far out of town before he meets his demise.
		* Session 23: A Troll leader named **[[Hargulka]]** is trying to establish his own kingdom and ordering his trolls to attack nearby settlements of Freehaven.  The PCs defeat Hargulka and reclaim the lair he had occupied which was an old dwarven stone fortress.  Eventually, the lost dwarves who occupied this place start to return, including [[benedin]] who joins the PCs for a time in their adventures.  The dwarves join the kingdom and the town that grows around this old stone fort is called [[Bronzeshield Hold]].  As this is resolved, there is evidence that a mysterious green-haired fey woman ([[Nyrissa]]) might have had contact with Hargulka
			* [[RP4 Returning to Nightmarch after Hargulka's Fortress]] takes place here.
			* [[RP5 One Night in Nightmarch]] takes place here
		* Session 24: A **huge Owlbear** attacks a town in the kingdom and nearly destroys it.  The PCs track the Owlbear to its lair and engage in a very tough fight with it and the undead remains of its mate who had been killed.  The PCs find out that a hunter had been given a magic ring (by a green haired fey...[[Nyrissa]]) which entranced the huge beast and resulted in the death of its mate and therefore its rampage to try to destroy the town.
* Session 25
	* Also during this era of the troubles in the kingdom, the PCs continue to explore and have the following encounters:
		* They help settle a dispute between some loggers and the pixie [[Melianse]] who doesn't want the trees chopped down
		* The run across an expedition of nine gnome explorers under the leadership of [[Jubilost Narthropple]] who are attempting to map the region.  They save this expedition from going over a waterfall as they attempt to cross a river with their wagon.  Jubilost joins the kingdom
		* They fight a [[Hateful Hermit]]
		* They come across Elga Verniex, aka the [[The Old Beldame|Old Beldame]].  Everyone is sure she is a hag and are afraid of her.  The PCs end up determining that she has all the looks and words of a wicked creature, but none of the actions.  She eventually joins the kingdom and shares some of her elixir-making skills with some of them.
			* [[RP5dot5 Ekundayo's Feast]] takes place around this time.
* Session 30
	* **The Hunting Competition**
		* Our PCs were invited to a hunting competition thrown by a group called the Embeth Travelers led by [[Jamal Visser]].  Also invited were [[Annamede Belavarah]], a bard from from Pitax who is not a fan of [[King Irovetti]], and [[Edina Aldori]] and her sister [[Jolia Aldori]] who are originally from [[Restov]], but are at this time living in [[Varnhold]], the town founded by [[Maegar Varn]].
			* During the competition, the PCs come across Edina and Jolia who are struggling with a fight against a Wyvern.  Even with the PCs help, Jolia ends up being killed in that battle by the Wyvern.
			* When the competitors come together for a dinner at the conclusion, a "Bloom" happens in the middle of the dinning room of the lodge that they are eating at.  These bloom cause a plant to burst open expelling a monster into the area.  This was one of the first of many blooms that started happening in the kingdom.
	* **The Cult of the Bloom**
		* The PCs find during an investigation that the mysterious "Blooms" which cause plants to emit dangerous monsters is being initiated by a cult called the [[Cult of the Bloom]].  They found a scar in the barrier between the First World and our world in an area of the Stolen Lands and are exploiting it with a ritual to create these bloom.  They are trying to develop one monstrous bloom called the Cradle of Lamashtu.
		* The PCs go to where the cultists are trying to create the Cradle of Lamashtu and are able to close down the opening by battling the monsters there.
* Session 40
	* **Varnhold Vanishing**
		* In the town of [[Varnhold]], a treasure hunter named [[Willas Gunderson]] followed an ancient map and mistranslated it.  What he thought was an area described as having vast magical wealth was actually a warning of an area having vast magical danger because it is where the cyclops lich named [[Vordakai]] was entombed.  Willas went to the site and triggered the sequence which woke Vordakai up from his thousand year slumber.  When Vordakai realized that he had been asleep so long, he set about to finding out as much information as possible to try to restore his power.  One way he went about that was by abducting the entire town of Varnhold to extract all of their knowledge.
		* The PCs make a trip to Varnhold at [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]]'s request since she had heard of rumors of something very wrong there.  What they found was a  town entirely devoid of people.  It looked as if they had simply  dissappeared in the middle of going about their daily tasks.
		* After fighting off some Spriggan's who had taken up the fort at Varnhold when they found it empty, the PCs decide to follow some clues and go talk to the Nomen Centaurs to see if they can figure out what happened to Varnhold.
	*  **Encountering the Nomen**
		* The Nomen are initially very suspicious of the PCs because they have been treated very poorly by bipedal races in the past.  However, the PCs give back a prized bow called Skybolt that belongs to the Nomen tribe which the PCs found in Varnhold and this helps a little.
		* The PCs find out that the ruler [[Aecora|Aecora Silverfire]] is worried about her daughter, [[Xamanthe]] who has broken the Nomen taboo of entering the [[Valley of the Dead]] because she saw some mysterious shapes there.  Xamanthe has been missing several days and they are worried.
			* [[RP6 Reflections while traveling]] takes place here while traveling on the steppes of the [[Nomen Heights]] heading toward the [[Tors of Levenies]]
			* [[RP7 On the steps of the Tors of Levenies]] takes place here.

* Session 50
	* **Vordekais Tomb**
		* **Rescuing Xamanthe**  The PCs go into the Valley of the Dead and past it, they find [[Vordakai's Island]], a tall pillar in the middle of a bay.  They enter the island and end up finding Xamanthe, who has been captured by Vordakai and is being held hostage here.  They free her and have her go out of Vordakai's lair and wait for them while they deal with Vordakai.
			* [[RP9 In the Sepulchar Chamber]] takes place here
		* **Killing Vordekai**  After coming in contact with various horrors and challenges, including the dead of the town of Varnhold and the undead ghost of Willas Gunderson, the PCs finally find Vordakai in his lair.  He is very tough to defeat, but he thinks that he is mind controlling [[Lucas]] when really his mind control didn't work on Lucas and Lucas is fooling him.  This helps the PCs finally overcome him.  Once they kill him, they find  [[Vordakai's Eye-Gem]] -- a dangerous magical item.  They also find jars that hold the souls of Varnholds residents that haven't been killed yet and free them.  (Unfortunately, [[Maegar Varn]] had already been killed.)
		* Later, the town of Varnhold requests to join the kingdom of [[Freehaven]].
			* [[RP7 Patching up after the Undead Mage]] takes place here
		* **Returning Xamanthe**  The PCs return [[Xamanthe]] to the Nomen and they are very grateful, eventually adding their settlement to [[Freehaven]] as well.
			* [[RP10 Traveling back to the Nomen Heights with Xamanthe]] takes place here.
			* [[RP12 Once again traveling to Nomen Heights]] takes place here.

* Session 60
	* **The attack on Bronzeshield Hold**  
		* Shortly after the great feat, the PCs go to [[Bronzeshield Hold]].  While there, [[Ekundayo]]'s fiance [[Kisandra Numesti]] comes traveling quickly from [[Fort Drevlev]], which is a settlement started by [[Hannis Drevlev]].  She warns them that an army is coming toward Bronzeshield Hold to attack them.  It is an army of trolls from the [[Tiger Lords]] joined by an army of mercenaries sent by [[Pitax]].
		* Kisandra explains that Pitax, Drevlev and the Tiger Lords have joined in an alliance to defeat Freehaven.  However, the Tiger Lords forced Hannis Drevlev to offer up some hostages to prove he would be loyal.  Hannis, a selfish and evil man, told one of his generals, [[Terrion Numesti]] to give one of his daughters.  When Terrion refused, he was thrown into prison and Kisandra's sister was taken forcibly.  That is when Kisandra ran and came to warn Bronzeshield Hold and ask for help for her father and sister.
		* The PCs defeat the incoming army of trolls and mercenaries and then, after Bronzeshield Hold is safe, travel to Fort Drevlev to help Kisandra and to determine what is going on.
	* **[[Fort Drevlev]]**
		* The PCs infiltrate Fort Drevlev, by first taking up residence in an inn owned by [[Satinder Morne]], who is part of a resistance there.  Then, several of the PCs sign up for the Drevlev guard.  While part of this guard, they fight the Trolls that now roam the streets of Drevlev as well as other guards.  Eventually, they are able to attack Drevlev Keep where Hannis Drevlev resides.
		* In defeating Hannis Drevlev, the PCs also fight his brother in law, [[Imeckus Stroon]], who is a powerful magician and they encounter his wife, the Baroness [[Pavetta Stroon-Drevlev]].  The Baroness is a person of very powerful will and determination.  She gives the PCs advice and impresses them enough that they send her to Restov.
		* The PCs free [[Terrion Numesti]] but his daughter had been hauled off by the [[Tiger Lords]] and their leader [[Armag Reborn]]
			* [[RP16 Drevlev Infiltrations]] takes place here.
* Session 65
	* **The Tiger-Lords and the Twice-Born Warlord**
		* The leader of the [[Tiger Lords]] believes he is the reborn incarnation of an ancient warlord called Armag.  Armag of old was defeated and prophecies said that the person who can retrieve his powerful magical sword, [[Ovinrbaane]]
		* The PCs find the Tiger Lords -- they are in the vault where the old Armag's sword is kept.  As the PCs join the scene, Armag and his forces have defeated the people who defend the vault and Ovinrbaane and he has claimed the sword as his own, after which he kills his own henchmen.  The PCs defeat him, rescue the kidnapped daughter and return home.
		*  [[RP17 Long road home]] takes place here
* Session 70
	* **The Nightmarch Games**
		* The PCs decide to invite the surrounding kingdoms into Nightmarch to hold a competition in several different events (like jousting, fighting, story telling).  Many nations come, including Pitax with [[King Irovetti]].  
		* Pitax takes the lead early on in the competition, but it all comes down to the last event when [[Riven]] makes an amazing shot in the archery competition to put Freehaven into the win. 
		* However, right as the competition ends, Pitax launches an attack on Freehaven.  They had been using the games as a cover for positioning their best people in Freehaven.  This begins the war between Freehaven and Pitax.
		* This is also the event where the PCs meet [[Eri Malai]] who is going by the name of his missing master, [[Saint Flint]]
			* [[RP18 Word from Afar]] takes place here before the Rushlight games

* Session 80
	* **Infiltration of Pitax**
		* The PCs sneak into Pitax by joining a group making a shipment from the north.  Then they start making contacts in the city.  Some people that turn out to be helpful are [[Annamede Belavarah]], whom they knew from the hunting competition, [[Atlee Quinge]], who directs a theater troop called the Red Crescent Troop, [[Gaspere Liacenza]], who is the leader of the Liacenza Bandit House, [[Jhofre Vascari]], leader of the Vascari Bandit House.
		* The PCs, with the help of several of these people, particularly Gaspere, begin an underground movement within Pitax knows as the Fox Alliance.  Through this alliance, they distribute communications about an uprising and the overthrow of King Irovetti.
		* Finally, the PCs get into Irovetti's palace and are able to defeat him (Pavel's strategy of following him with an aura of silence is particularly useful).  However, there are further challenges once they find his secret bedroom.  Once they clear out the monsters there, they find [[Briar]], the sword that hold [[Nyrissa]]'s capacity for love, which was forged as a punishment and hidden from her.  Irovetti had found it and had not told Nyrissa.
			* [[RP26 Riven's Journey to Kyonin]] takes place here
			* [[RP21 The Mysterious Disappearance of St. Flint's Master.]] takes place here
* Session 90
	* **Deep into Candlemere** and the Threshold of Dread
		* After Irovetti's death and the conclusion of the war with Pitax, the PCs get word of strange developments on Candlemere island, so they decide to investigate.  Their investigation leads them to find out the way to open the seal on the second floor of the dungeon under Candlemere, so they open it an decide to descend
		* This begins a long, tough battle with the cult that has been trapped in the lower dungeon levels and the undead priests who performed the ritual to trap the cultists in this dungeon (and did so knowing that they would end up trapping themselves as well.)  Both groups attacked ruthlessly.
		* The PCs almost lost their lives, and, at one point, [[Pavel]] has to drop [[Briar]] to survive.  But, they go back and retrieve [[Briar]] and eventually made it through the battle (it helped that Riven joined them near the end).
			* [[RP23 Deep Under Candlemere]] takes place here
