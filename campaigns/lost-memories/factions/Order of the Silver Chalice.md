# Order of the Silver Chalice

> **Type**: Magical order / council
> **Reach**: Spans all kingdoms
> **Status**: Active — unaware of Aldric's activities

## Overview

An ancient, powerful, and politically influential magical order created by good wizards to foster peace across kingdoms. Its membership comprises the most powerful mages in the known world. The Order serves as a neutral body — part diplomatic institution, part magical authority — that operates above the politics of any single kingdom.

Each senior member holds the title of **Magister** and is assigned a wing in the Order's central hall *(location TBD)*.

## The Order's Public Role

- Mediates disputes between kingdoms
- Regulates dangerous magical practice
- Maintains a balance of power that prevents any single ruler from dominating the others

## The Rot Inside

[[Magister Aldric]] is using his position within the Order as a launchpad for a secret bid for total power. He intends to make himself powerful enough to seize control of the Order itself — and through the Order's political reach, control the known world. His colleagues believe him to be a difficult but legitimate peer.

One unnamed rival Magister has grown suspicious of Aldric. Whether this person is an ally for the PCs or a threat in their own right is TBD.

## Key Members

- *Unnamed rival Magister* — suspicious of Aldric; details TBD
