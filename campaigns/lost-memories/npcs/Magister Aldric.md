# Magister Aldric

> **Role**: Antagonist
> **Location**: [[The Wizard's Prison]] *(secret tower); also [[Order of the Silver Chalice]] central hall (~60% of time)*
> **Faction**: [[Order of the Silver Chalice]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A pudgy, unremarkable-looking man of about 50. Not physically imposing — the kind of person who gets overlooked in a crowd. His appearance disarms people, and he knows it. His voice and manner were once controlled and measured; since the ritual failed, there is a brittleness beneath the surface. He can shift from clipped precision to volatile frustration without warning.

Extraordinarily gifted with magic. His talent is the one thing about him that has never been in question.

## Personality

Power-hungry and amoral, but not yet fully monstrous. He does not see people as individuals — only as resources with measurable value. Capable of great patience when a plan is working; volatile and erratic when it isn't. The failed ritual has left him obsessive: simultaneously fascinated by the memory-loss side effect and furious that the power transference didn't work.

**What he wants**: To seize control of the [[Order of the Silver Chalice]] and, through it, the known world. To do that, he needs to be significantly more powerful than every other Magister and every king and ruler in every kingdom — which is why he invented the [[The Power Transference Ritual|Power Transference Ritual]].

**What he fears**: That the ritual is fundamentally flawed and the power he wants is unreachable. That his colleagues in the Order will discover what he's doing before he's strong enough to act.

**What he's hiding**: The ritual has never worked. The PCs are not the first subjects. His secret tower and everything that happens in it.

**His restraint**: He hasn't killed any of his subjects — yet. He still views them as investments, not waste. That line is moving.

## Background

A lifelong member of the [[Order of the Silver Chalice]], where he has risen to the rank of Magister. Grown deeply impatient with the slow accumulation of power through study and practice. Became convinced that power accrued through lived mastery could be extracted and transferred ritually — bypassing decades of work. Has spent years developing the [[The Power Transference Ritual|Power Transference Ritual]] in secret.

He lives in one of the kingdoms *(TBD which)* but spends roughly 60% of his time at the Order's central hall, where he has been assigned a wing. His colleagues know him as a capable, if increasingly temperamental, peer.

## Artifacts

The failed ritual dispersed the PCs' memories into objects dear to Aldric. Examples:
- A powerful magic item he obtained on a dangerous quest — his most prized possession
- A sentimental gift he received as a child, still at his mother's house; he hasn't seen it in years

Neither Aldric nor the PCs know the memories ended up in these objects yet. *(Discovery mechanism TBD.)*

## Relationships

- The PCs — subjects/prisoners; valuable experimental material, and now a dangerous liability if they escape
- [[Order of the Silver Chalice]] colleagues — peers who believe him to be one of them
- One unnamed rival Magister — suspicious of Aldric; details and trustworthiness TBD
- His mother — estranged or simply distant; her home contains one of the artifact vessels without her knowledge
