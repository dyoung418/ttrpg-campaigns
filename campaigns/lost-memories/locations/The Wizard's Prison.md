# The Wizard's Prison

> **Region**: Desolate coast — isolated, remote, no settlements in sight
> **Type**: Wizard's tower — multi-level, purpose-built for experimentation and confinement
> **Status**: The PCs are imprisoned here at the start of the campaign

## Description

A tall, multi-level tower built in secret by [[Magister Aldric]] on a desolate stretch of coastline. This is not his official residence — it is entirely off the books, unknown to his colleagues in the [[Order of the Silver Chalice]]. The PCs wake here at the start of Session 1 with no memory of how they arrived.

The coast outside is grey and empty. The isolation is complete — escape from the tower puts the PCs on an unfamiliar shore with no clear direction toward civilization.

## Containment

A mix of physical locks, human mercenary guards, and magical wards. Not impenetrable — the guards have daily routines with exploitable weaknesses. Aldric has not designed it for a determined escape attempt; he has designed it for people who don't know who they are.
