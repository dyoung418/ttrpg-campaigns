# The Golden Talons

> **Type**: Military unit — Pitax standing army
> **Status**: Defeated (Pitax War concluded; [[King Irovetti|Irovetti]] dead)

## Summary

Two battalions of [[Pitax]]'s professional military, distinguished by [[King Irovetti|Irovetti]]'s golden goblet livery. During the Pitax War, they were tasked with a flanking assault on [[Silverstep]] — a scholarly lakeside town poorly defended relative to [[Freehaven]]'s capital.

The intelligence that the Golden Talons were marching on [[Silverstep]] was extracted by [[Riven]] from a terrified supply wagon driver during a road ambush, and passed to [[Tovan]]: *"They outnumber her two to one. We must reach her before they do."* The "her" in question was [[Cassandra]], Silverstep Guard captain.

The vanguard — sixty mercenaries under [[Lord Darian]] — arrived at the Silverstep meadow and engaged [[Cassandra]]'s twenty-man Guard at the hedgerow ditch. Three Guards killed, five wounded; Guard withdrew in order. Lord Darian held the field and waited for the remaining battalions.

The full force of two hundred marched at dawn. [[Riven]] had arrived overnight with thirty Rangers and planned the [[Willowshade]] trap with Cassandra: feigned retreat across the ford, Rangers on the ridges, druid storm. Darian drove his men too deep into the hollow, mistaking retreat for collapse. Officers fell from the ridges. The blizzard shattered discipline. The Guard wheeled and blocked the valley's throat. Darian was killed by a flaming arrow through the breastplate. The Talons broke — two hundred men dissolved into fugitives. The Golden Talons ceased to exist as a fighting force.

## Leadership

- [[Lord Darian]] — vanguard commander; mounted; white plume; contemptuous and methodical

## Goals

1. Assault and take [[Silverstep]] as part of the coordinated Pitax War offensive
2. Exploit the town's soft defenses — mercenaries on the convoy were already anticipating easy pickings from "scholars and scribes"

## Methods

Professional military march, coordinated with the broader Pitax offensive that used the [[Grand Tournament of Freehaven]] as a distraction to hit multiple Freehaven cities simultaneously.

## Relationships to Other Factions

- [[Freehaven]] — enemy
