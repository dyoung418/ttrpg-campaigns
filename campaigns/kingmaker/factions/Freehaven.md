> **Alignment**:
> **Status**: Active

![[freehaven-flag.png]]
## Summary

Freehaven is the kingdom founded by the PCs in the Stolen Lands, beginning from a royal charter granted by [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]] of Brevoy. What started as a small barony formed after defeating the [[Stag Lord]] has grown into a recognized kingdom spanning the Stolen Lands. Its governance is an oligarchy led by the PCs themselves.

## Major Settlements

- [[Nightmarch]] — Capital

- [[Serenko]] — Industrial hub; stone masons and lumberyards

- [[Bronzeshield Hold]] — Reclaimed dwarven stronghold
- [[Ashenfel]] — Nomen Centaur town
- [[Pitax]] — Former rival capital, absorbed after Irovetti's defeat
- [[Fort Drevlev]] — Former Drevlev settlement, absorbed after his defeat

## Origins

Founded after the PCs defeated the [[Stag Lord]] and received the right to establish a barony from [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]]. Grew through years of exploration, diplomacy, and conflict into a kingdom spanning the full Stolen Lands region.

## Goals

1. Defend and grow the kingdom against threats (currently: [[Nyrissa]] and the blooms)
2. Maintain stability and prosperity for all citizens, including integrated communities

## Relationships to Other Factions

- [[Nomen Centaurs]] — allied; joined Freehaven after the PCs rescued the ruler's daughter from [[Vordakai]]
- [[Nyrissa]] — existential threat; she seeks to absorb the entire kingdom as the 1,000th grain in the Apology
