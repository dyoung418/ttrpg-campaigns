# Cult of the Bloom

> **Type**: Cult
> **Alignment**: Chaotic Evil
> **Status**: Defeated (this incursion closed by the PCs)

## Summary

A cult that discovered a scar in the barrier between the First World and the mortal world within the Stolen Lands, and exploited it through ritual to create the "Blooms" — plants that burst open to expel monsters into the kingdom.

## Goals

1. Exploit the First World scar to spread Blooms across the region
2. Cultivate one monstrous Bloom, the **Cradle of Lamashtu**

## Methods

Ritual manipulation of the planar scar to trigger Blooms. The first such Bloom struck at the dinner concluding the Hunting Competition (Session 30), erupting in the middle of the lodge's dining room.

## History

The PCs traced the Blooms to the cult during an investigation, then traveled to the site where the cultists were trying to birth the Cradle of Lamashtu and shut the opening by destroying the monsters there (Session 30).
