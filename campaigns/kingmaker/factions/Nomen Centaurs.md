# Nomen Centaurs

> **Type**: Tribal people
> **Status**: Allied with Freehaven

## Summary

A centaur people living in the Stolen Lands. After the PCs rescued one of the Nomen ruler's daughters from the evil lich lord [[Vordakai]], the Nomens pledged loyalty to [[Freehaven]] and their town of [[Ashenfel]] was integrated into the kingdom.

## Base of Operations

[[Ashenfel]]

## Key NPCs

- [[Aecora]] — leader; fiercely loyal and protective of her people
- [[Xamanthe]] — Aecora's daughter; captured by Vordakai, rescued by the party; pledged a debt to them

## Culture & Traditions

- **Spring festival**: includes a race; the kinderfoal division is for the youngest (and, apparently, two-legged guests who want a fighting chance)
- Vordakai is a figure of legend to the Nomen Centaurs — described as a "boogeyman of their society." The reality was worse than their legends told; Xamanthe expressed deep fear about what brought him back after 100,000 years and how her herd will survive such a threat going forward.

## Relationships to Other Factions

- [[Freehaven]] — allied; member community
