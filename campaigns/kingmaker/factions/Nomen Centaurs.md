# Nomen Centaurs

> **Type**: Tribal people
> **Status**: Allied with Freehaven

## Summary

A centaur people living in the Stolen Lands. After the PCs rescued one of the Nomen ruler's daughters from the evil lich lord [[Vordakai]], the Nomens pledged loyalty to [[Freehaven]] and their town of [[Ashenfel]] was integrated into the kingdom.
