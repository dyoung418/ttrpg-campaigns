# Nagaji Monastic Order

*(Actual name unknown — flagged for GM. "Nagaji Monastic Order" is a working descriptor.)*

> **Type**: Religious / monastic order
> **Status**: Active — re-emerged after Irovetti's fall

## Summary

A nagaji monastic order that operated inside [[Pitax]] during [[King Irovetti]]'s rule. When their leader, [[Saint Flint]], was forced to disappear to protect them from Irovetti's reprisals, they went into hiding among the people rather than disband. They were leaderless but not broken.

In her absence, [[Eri Malai]] — one of her students — took her name, her robes, and her staff, and led the order as best he could, believing he was following her unspoken instructions.

After Irovetti's defeat, the order came out of hiding. [[Saint Flint]] returned shortly after, having learned of Irovetti's death from a traveling merchant rather than from her student casting Guiding Star.

## Leadership

- [[Saint Flint]] — restored leader; the original and only Saint Flint
- [[Eri Malai]] — senior student; acting leader in the Master's absence; now subordinate again

## Base of Operations

[[Pitax]] (formerly underground; now operating openly)

## Goals

1. Practice and propagate their monastic tradition among the nagaji people
2. Protect their community from political pressure and religious persecution

## Relationships to Other Factions

- [[Freehaven]] — allied by circumstance; [[Eri Malai]] joined the PCs against Irovetti
