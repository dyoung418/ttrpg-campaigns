# Carandini Family

> **Type**: Woodland-elf family line
> **Alignment**:
> **Status**: Active

## Summary

The Carandini are a large woodland-elf family, notorious even among themselves for their sheer size and for a naming tradition kept up half out of custom and half out of pure stubbornness: every one of their nineteen children is named for the next consecutive letter of the alphabet followed by "-ee" — Aee, Bee, [[Cee]], Dee, Eee, and on down the line. [[Lee]], the party's General of [[Freehaven]], is the twelfth-born and left the family to make a name that was his alone.

## Leadership

- [[Aee]] — eldest of the nineteen children; now heads the family

- [[Lee]] — twelfth-born; left home to seek fame, now General of [[Freehaven]] (no contact since departing)
- [[Cad]] — sixteenth-born; goes by "Cad" rather than his letter-name
