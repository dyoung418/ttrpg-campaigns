# Tiger Lords

> **Type**: Barbarian tribe / troll warband
> **Alignment**: Chaotic Evil
> **Status**: Defeated

## Summary

A barbarian people led by [[Armag Reborn]], who believes himself the reincarnation of the ancient warlord Armag. Allied with [[Pitax]] and [[Hannis Drevlev]] against [[Freehaven]], fielding trolls alongside their warriors.

## Leadership

- [[Armag Reborn]] — warlord; claimed the legendary sword [[Ovinrbaane]] from the old Armag's vault

## Goals

1. Reclaim Armag's legacy and the sword [[Ovinrbaane]]
2. Crush [[Freehaven]] as part of the alliance with Pitax and Drevlev

## Methods

Brute force and intimidation. To secure [[Hannis Drevlev]]'s loyalty in the alliance they demanded hostages; when Drevlev's general [[Terrion Numesti]] refused to surrender a daughter, they took [[Kisandra Numesti]]'s sister captive by force.

## History

- Joined [[Pitax]] and [[Hannis Drevlev]] in alliance against Freehaven
- Marched trolls and mercenaries on [[Bronzeshield Hold]] (Session 60); the army was broken by the PCs
- The PCs tracked them to the vault holding old Armag's sword, where [[Armag Reborn]] had already claimed [[Ovinrbaane]] and slain his own henchmen; the PCs defeated him and rescued the kidnapped daughter (Session 65)
