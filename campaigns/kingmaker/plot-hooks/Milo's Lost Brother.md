# Milo's Lost Brother

**Status**: Open — the wound at the center of Milo's story

## Summary

[[Milo]] had an older brother named [[Rindle]]. While the two were traveling together to collect rare materials for their inventions, they were ambushed by bandits. Rindle fought to protect Milo but was captured and dragged away. Milo was left beaten and alone, helpless to stop it.

Milo doesn't know if Rindle is alive. He has never stopped wanting to find him and bring the bandits to justice.

He built his mechanical construct and named it Rindle — a walking reminder of what he's working toward.

## The Questions

- **Is Rindle still alive?** He was taken captive, not killed. But that was long ago.
- **Who were the bandits?** Are they identifiable? Connected to any faction the party has encountered?
- **Where is Rindle now?** Sold as a slave? Pressed into service? Escaped? Something worse?
- **Does Rindle know Milo is looking for him?** If he's alive, has he tried to get word out?
- **What does Milo plan to do when he finds the bandits?** He wants justice — but what does that mean to him?

## Possible Developments

- **A lead surfaces** — a bandit group in the Stolen Lands matches the description; a prisoner knows something
- **Word from [[Glimmerhaven]]** — someone has news about Rindle, good or bad
- **Rindle finds Milo** — after all this time, Rindle tracks down his brother; what has he become?
- **[[Gelbin Steambender]] knows more** — Gelbin may have information about the bandits or Rindle's fate that he's never mentioned
- **The construct Rindle connects** — something about the construct triggers a lead on the real Rindle

## Key NPCs

- [[Milo]] — the one driving this; hasn't given up
- [[Rindle]] — the missing brother; status unknown
- [[Gelbin Steambender]] — Milo's mentor; may know more than he's said
