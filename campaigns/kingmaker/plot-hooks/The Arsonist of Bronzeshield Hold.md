# The Arsonist of Bronzeshield Hold

**Status**: Open — active threat during party's absence

## Summary

Someone — referred to by [[Benedin]] as "the red-haired devil" — has been committing arson at [[Bronzeshield Hold]]. The timing suggests they grew bolder while the party was away dealing with the Vordakai threat. Benedin plans to stay home to watch for them.

## What's Known

- The arsonist has red hair — the only identifying detail Benedin has given
- They are bold enough to operate even with a dwarven community present
- Benedin considers a few rituals that might snare them, but worries about accidentally catching innocent townsfolk instead

## Open Questions

- Who is the red-haired arsonist? What is their motive?
- Are they connected to the night demons [[Benedin]] has been investigating?
- Is this personal (targeting dwarves specifically), political (destabilizing Freehaven), or something else?

## Key NPCs

- [[Benedin]] — the one tracking this; he plans to stay at Bronzeshield while the party handles Nyrissa
- The arsonist — unnamed, red-haired, unknown affiliation

## Possible Developments

- Benedin catches them alone — or they catch him first
- The arsonist is connected to the *Eclipsed Incantations* night demons somehow
- A larger faction is using the arsonist to destabilize Freehaven while the party is occupied
