# Grand Tournament of Freehaven

**Status**: Resolved — ended in war; Pitax defeated; now part of Freehaven

## Summary

[[Riven]] announced the Grand Tournament at the Great Feast of Freehaven. It has since drawn delegations from across the River Kingdoms: **Tymon, Gralton, Daggermark, Mivon, and Pitax**, alongside Freehaven as host. Nightmarch is at a cosmopolitan peak — the gates swarming with traders, caravan guards, and nobles in lacquered carriages. Freehaven's colors (green, blue, black, and white) snap from every rampart.

[[King Irovetti]] of Pitax attended personally and attempted to bet on the outcome.

## Events and Results

| Event | Status | Result |
|---|---|---|
| **Archery Contest** | Complete | [[Riven]] — gold; Pitax — dead last (despite cheating) |
| **Test of the Axe** | Upcoming | Freehaven strategy: recruit from lumber mill |
| **Midnight Joust** | Upcoming (final event) | [[Pavel]] representing Freehaven, riding [[Cat]] |

**Pitax's cheating at the Archery Contest**: Riven observed spells being cast and adamantine arrows being used. Pitax still finished dead last. Astor learned about the arrows from the groundskeeper and kept it quiet — Riven won cleanly and calling out Pitax would muddy that. Riven's own arrows are identifiable (all in the green target; three in the bullseye).

## What Irovetti Was Actually Planning

Pavel was right: Irovetti didn't care about the tournament results. The tournament was a trap.

At the final event — with all of Freehaven's rulers gathered in one place — [[King Irovetti]] launched a coordinated military assault on Freehaven's cities. The tournament was cover to concentrate the kingdom's leadership and leave the borders exposed. **This was the beginning of an all-out war.**

**[[Grigori]]** is a precedent the party already knew: a prior Pitax envoy who had caused significant trouble inside Freehaven — they had been watching for exactly this kind of scheme.

## Freehaven's Strategy

- **Test of the Axe**: Astor is recruiting from Nightmarch's lumber mill — the first industry established over a decade ago. Lumberjacks know tricks warriors don't; they might compete directly or coach a Freehaven representative.
- **Midnight Joust**: Pavel + Cat. *"In a fair contest, I'd put my money on Cat eleven times in a tenday."* The question is how Pitax cheats.

## The War and Irovetti's Defeat

**Resolution**: [[Riven]] remained in Freehaven to lead the kingdom's armies against the Pitax invasion. The rest of the party infiltrated [[Pitax]] itself and overthrew [[King Irovetti]] from within — aided by **[[Gaspere Liacenza]]**, a Pitaxian bard who turned against his king. Pitax fell. The kingdom was absorbed into Freehaven.

**Status**: Resolved — Irovetti is dead, the war is over, Pitax is part of Freehaven.
