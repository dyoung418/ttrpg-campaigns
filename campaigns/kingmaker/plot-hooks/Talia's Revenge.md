# Talia's Revenge

**Status**: Active — latent threat that could arrive at any time

## Summary

[[Talia]], sister of [[Rowan Half Dark]] and current leader of [[The Few]], has placed a standing hit on [[Riven]]'s life for killing Rowan. Riven fled the River Kingdoms ahead of the Few's pursuit, but the vendetta has not ended.

Riven is now the Ruler of [[Freehaven]] — a king. If Talia doesn't already know this, she will discover it eventually. The question is what she does with that information.

## Tension Points

- **Assassination**: Talia sends agents into Freehaven posing as merchants, travelers, or petitioners — targeting Riven directly.
- **Political leverage**: She threatens to expose Riven's past (the village incident, the banditry, killing Rowan) to destabilize his rule or pressure him into a meeting.
- **Open challenge**: As Riven's profile grows, Talia escalates to public confrontation — demanding justice for her brother before the kingdom.
- **Blackmail over Freehaven**: She targets not just Riven but people he cares about.

## Key NPCs

- [[Talia]] — driving the vendetta
- [[Rowan Half Dark]] — the death she's avenging
- [[Riven]] — the target
- [[The Few]] — the instrument of revenge

## GM Questions

- Does Talia believe Riven was wrong to kill Rowan, or does she know Rowan crossed a line and is acting on grief regardless?
- Is this vendetta truly about justice, or about Talia's own grief and need for purpose?
- Could Riven ever speak to her honestly about why he killed Rowan — and would she listen?
