# The Month of Destruction

Part 1 of [[Chapter 10 - Sound of a Thousand Screams]]. A single month, one Kingdom turn, during which **no kingdom management or development can occur**. Eight bloom events strike over 30 days.

## How Blooms Work

- Each bloom appears in a specific hex type; the capital hex is never chosen.
- The bloom **loses the hex** from the kingdom. Spreads 1 hex/day into adjacent unbloomed hexes.
- Defeating a bloom: hex stays lost but must be **reclaimed**. All spread hexes revert.
- Defeating a bloom drops bloom trophies — items magically linked to a specific glade in [[Thousandbreaths]]. Needed to open the Castle of Knives portal (need **at least 3 different types**).
- Bloom Trophy effect: +1 status bonus on attack rolls, weapon damage, skill checks, saves, and ability checks while in the linked glade.

## Key Tactical Notes

**Nights of Dread (Day 12)** is the most insidious bloom — doesn't change terrain but kills citizens nightly with escalating living nightmares. PCs must confront the Nightmare Rook in their dreams. The DC starts at 40 and drops by 2 on each Success. See living nightmares table (d8 roll: baleful polymorph, blindness, dominate, feeblemind, flesh to stone, phantasmal killer, polar ray, warp mind).

**War of the High Folly (Day 26)** besieges the capital. Army attacks 1 hour before sunset; must Endure Bloom Siege (DC 34 Defense or Magic check, +2/day) each sundown until bloom ends. Win the war encounter or kill the sard to end it.

**Day 28** — No bloom, just the jabberwock. It only appears if the PCs haven't entered Thousandbreaths already. Great incentive to move on Part 2.

## Notice Bloom Rules

Secret kingdom skill check when a bloom manifests:
- **Unclaimed hex**: DC 35 Exploration check
- **Claimed hex**: DC 30 Defense check (improve 1 degree if settlement present)
- **Capital or occupied by PC**: Auto crit success

**Bloom Watch** downtime activity: DC 40 Defense/Survival, DC 36 Intrigue/Perception (expert), or DC 32 Magic/Nature (master) to find undiscovered blooms.

## Unbloomed Bonus

If PCs enter Thousandbreaths before a bloom manifests in the Stolen Lands, the corresponding glade becomes a **Severe 18 encounter** instead of Moderate 18. Defeating all glade guardians before a bloom's scheduled day prevents it from appearing.
