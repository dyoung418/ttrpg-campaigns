# Almon's Hunt for Cassandra

> **Status**: Transformed — the original target is dead; Almon himself remains unaccounted for
> **Threat level**: Unknown

## Summary

During the Pitax War, [[Almon d'Avoine]] — a Pitax intelligence operative embedded in a supply convoy — survived [[Riven]]'s Ranger ambush by feigning death. He overheard [[Riven]] name [[Cassandra]] as his priority target, and filed her away as his intelligence objective.

**[[Cassandra]] died at the [[Willowshade]] valley ford**, killed by a Pitax spear during the battle that broke the [[Golden Talons]]. Almon, wherever he is, does not yet know this. The war has ended with [[King Irovetti|Irovetti]] dead and [[Pitax]] absorbed into [[Freehaven]].

## What Almon Had

- Cassandra's name and strategic significance to Riven
- Knowledge that she commanded [[Silverstep]]'s Guard
- The face of no one — he saw Riven's Rangers from beneath a dead man's cloak

## What Changed

Cassandra is dead. Almon's original target no longer exists. But Almon himself — a trained intelligence operative who survived a battle by thinking faster than everyone else — is still out there with:
- Detailed knowledge of Freehaven's military command during the war
- An understanding of Riven's priorities and decision-making under pressure
- No known ties to the now-defeated Pitax, which makes him unpredictable

## How the Hook Transforms

The question is no longer "will Almon reach Cassandra?" — it's "what does Almon do next?"

Possibilities:
- He finds a new angle: the intelligence about Riven's decision-making is still valuable to Freehaven's enemies
- He sells his knowledge to whoever opposes Freehaven after Pitax's fall
- He goes quiet — a sleeper; surfaces years later
- He learns Cassandra died heroically and files that away too

## Hook Triggers

- An enemy of Freehaven acquires suspiciously specific intelligence about how Riven leads
- A man no one recognizes keeps appearing near figures close to Riven
- Someone asks Freehaven questions that imply they know more than they should about the Pitax War's field decisions

## Characters Involved

- [[Almon d'Avoine]] — the unknown variable
- [[Cassandra]] — deceased; was his target; her death closes that thread
- [[Riven]] — the reason Almon has any intelligence at all; doesn't know Almon exists
