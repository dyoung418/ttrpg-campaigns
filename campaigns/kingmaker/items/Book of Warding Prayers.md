# Book of Warding Prayers

*The Sato family's sacred history and prayer book; in [[Lucas]]'s possession*

> **Status**: In Lucas's possession — he carries it; it is well-used
> **Source**: Recovered from [[Hargulka]]'s lair

## Description

A tome containing the Sato (kitsune) family's sacred history going back generations. Includes warding prayers, ancestral records, and ritual knowledge. Lucas has carried it since recovering it from [[Hargulka]]'s lair during the troll campaign.

When the ancestor vision produced a potential wedding between [[Great Aunt Sumi]] and [[Great Uncle Olaf]], Lucas immediately reached for this book — either to look up appropriate gifts for ancestor marriages, or to find a warding against the situation. Possibly both.

## Significance

- A rare surviving record of the Sato family's history — particularly meaningful given that kitsune kept to the shadows in the Stolen Lands for generations, leaving few written traces
- Contains prayers and rituals that Lucas understands and uses
- May contain information relevant to the kitsune history in [[Nightmarch]] (pre-dating even [[Great Great Aunt Gina]]'s time)
