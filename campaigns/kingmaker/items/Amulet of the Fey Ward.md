# Amulet of the Fey Ward

> **Type**: Wondrous (neck)
> **Location**: [[Ilthuliak]]'s hoard, [[Glade J — The High Folly]]
> **Status**: Unclaimed (dragon-gated)

## Summary

A fey-made amulet, old and faintly warm to the touch — it *feels* protective. Hidden in [[Ilthuliak]]'s hoard. The party won't automatically know its specific power: it **wards its wearer against the [[House at the Edge of Time]]'s teleport trap** (Secret 7 of [[Session 102 - The Undefeated]]). Let them discover what it does when it matters, not before.

**Dragon-gated** — only recoverable if the party reaches and defeats Ilthuliak at the High Folly. May slide to a later session if the fight runs long.
