# Eclipsed Incantations

*A powerful magical tome; currently in [[Benedin]]'s possession and study*

## Known Contents

**Binding Circle Ritual** — [[Benedin]] believes this may explain the "night demons" plaguing [[Bronzeshield Hold]]. He also notes it could be repurposed to summon more benevolent spirits rather than ward against hostile ones.

**Permanent Imprisonment Ritual** — The stronger of the two. A way to trap someone or something forever — not killing them, but imprisoning them indefinitely. Benedin described it conspiratorially: *"Most everything has a weakness, but some things just can't die. This is a way to put them away, for good."* [[Pavel]] called the concept "a fate worse than death."

## Significance

- Most of the tome is beyond Benedin's current understanding — he's still working through it
- The imprisonment ritual could be relevant against enemies who cannot be killed (undead, demons, powerful fey)
- Could intersect with the [[Nyrissa]] endgame — she is a fey with deep ties to existence itself
