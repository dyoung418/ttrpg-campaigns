# Fox-Head Pendant

*A carved pendant that grants a bonus to Charisma.*

## What It Is

A pendant carved in the shape of an animal's head — canonically a **wolf's head**, but [[Lucas]] (a kitsune) immediately reflavored it as a **fox head** to suit himself. It grants a bonus to his Charisma.

## Current Wielder

**[[Lucas]]** — recovered it from the loot of [[Ilthuliak]]'s keep and now wears it. The fox-head reading is his; it fits his heritage and his role as Freehaven's Magister and face.

## How the Party Acquired It

Part of the haul looted from [[Ilthuliak]]'s keep after the dragon fell (recapped at the top of [[Session 103 - The Shape of Where I Used to Be|Session 103]]; distributed to the party during that session).
