# Lee

> **Class / Race**: Woodland Elf — Swashbuckler (Aldori Duelist dedication)
> **Level**: 17
> **Kingdom Role**: General of [[Freehaven]]
> **Full Name**: Lee Carandini

## Backstory Hook

Sword Scion background — trained in the Aldori dueling tradition. His Aldori Duelist dedication reflects a formal commitment to that school.

Lee is the twelfth of nineteen children born to [[Erevan Carandini|Erevan]] and [[Elithya Carandini|Elithya]] Carandini, woodland elves who — half out of tradition, half out of sheer stubbornness — decided every child they had would take the next letter of the alphabet followed by "ee": Aee, Bee, Cee, Dee, Eee, etc. By the time Lee arrived there were already eleven siblings ahead of him and seven more would follow — a household where being memorable took real effort.

[[Aee]], the eldest, now heads the [[Carandini Family]]. [[Cee]], third-born and the family's Court Arcanist, spent much of Lee's childhood needling him with illusions and minor hexes dressed up as "lessons" — cantrips flashed just to watch him flinch, tricks explained only after he'd already been humiliated by them. Lee walked away from that childhood with a private, unshakeable conviction: magic isn't real, not in the way anyone claims — it's a con, and every spellcaster is in on the same joke, dressing up sleight-of-hand and misdirection as cosmic law. He never says this out loud. As a duelist who trades in feints, bluffs, and theatrical misdirection himself, he recognizes a fellow performer's craft when he sees one, and it would be bad form — bad _game_ — to call another professional's bluff in public. Privately, though, he's never once been talked out of it.

Somewhere in the middle of nineteen children, with a family that already had a head, a mage, and no shortage of everything else, Lee decided the only title left worth having was one nobody else in the Carandini line could claim. He set out from home to make a name loud enough that "twelfth of nineteen" would stop being the first thing anyone said about him.

## Motivations

- **To be undeniably his own.** Growing up as one of nineteen, recognition was scarce and had to be fought for; Lee left home to build a reputation that belongs to him alone, not one measured against a sibling.
- **To outshine [[Cee]], specifically.** Beneath the ambition is an old, unresolved score — being the sibling who proves that swordplay, nerve, and showmanship beat "magic" (which he still privately considers a very convincing trick).
- **Fame and glory for their own sake.** Even setting the family politics aside, Lee genuinely wants to be a legend — the kind of name sung in taverns and put on wanted posters and playbills alike.

## Character Arc

Left the Carandini estate to seek fame as a blade for hire, trading on Aldori dueling pedigree and a talent for theater as much as swordplay. Found his way into the mercenary company that would go on to found [[Freehaven]], and rose alongside the rest of the party from landless adventurers to the kingdom's ruling council — ending up General, a title with real weight and real recognition, the kind that doesn't need to be shouted about at a family dinner to matter.

Lee has never made contact with the [[Carandini Family]] since leaving — no letters, no word sent home. Whether news of a Carandini son commanding a kingdom's armies has reached [[Aee]] and [[Cee]] regardless is unknown to him. Worth revisiting once his family reappears as an active thread.

## Personality & Quirks

- Showman in combat: prefers flashy feints, daring stunts, and theatrics to gain Panache — winning with style matters as much as winning.
- A pathological liar who relishes every chance to deceive — not just as a combat tactic, but as a genuine pleasure. He'll spin a lie where the truth would do just as well, purely for the sport of it. Loyal to his friends regardless; the lying is aimed at everyone else, and even they know better than to take his word at face value.. except maybe [[Pavel]].
- Specializes in bleeding wounds and precision strikes at close range.
- Hates aerial or ranged foes — being kept out of reach is his most reliable frustration. Enemies who fly or kite him strip away his effectiveness and his dignity simultaneously.
- Along with [[Pavel]], he is one of the party members most often brought to death's door. The front line costs him.
- Privately convinced magic is an elaborate, victimless con and every spellcaster is in on it — a belief forged by years of his sister [[Cee]]'s "lessons." Never says this aloud; as a fellow purveyor of tricks and misdirection, he respects the bit too much to break it.

## Relationships

- [[Pavel]] — fellow front-liner; the two share the dubious honor of being the party's most-injured members
- [[Cee]] (sister, family Court Arcanist) — third of nineteen Carandini children; years of "teaching" Lee illusions at his own expense left him privately convinced all magic is a con
- [[Aee]] (sibling, head of the [[Carandini Family]]) — eldest of the nineteen; runs the family Lee left behind

## Family

The [[Carandini Family]] line: [[Erevan Carandini|Erevan]] and [[Elithya Carandini|Elithya]] Carandini (parents) had nineteen children, each named for a consecutive letter of the alphabet plus "-ee" — Aee, Bee, Cee, Dee, Eee, etc. [[Aee]] leads the family; [[Cee]] serves as its Court Arcanist. Lee is the twelfth-born.

- The sixteenth child likes to go by [[Cad]].
