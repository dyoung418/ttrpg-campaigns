# Milo

> **Player**: Billy
> **Class / Race**: Halfling (Gutsy Halfling heritage) — Inventor
> **Background**: Junk Collector
> **Level**: 17
> **Kingdom Role**: Treasurer of [[Freehaven]]
![[milo.png]]![[milo2.png]]
Milo's halfling heritage is evident in his physical features. He has warm, tan skin, giving him a healthy and outdoorsy complexion. His hair is a deep chestnut brown. Milo's most captivating feature is undoubtedly his bright, inquisitive eyes. They are a vibrant shade of emerald green, sparkling with curiosity and intelligence.

His attire is practical and designed for his adventurous lifestyle. Milo often wears a well-worn leather jacket with numerous pockets for carrying small tools and trinkets. His pants are durable and comfortable, allowing him to move freely as he explores the world and works on his inventions.

Milo's hands are his most important tools. His fingers are nimble and deft, capable of delicate work when repairing intricate mechanisms or crafting innovative devices. Scars and calluses on his hands serve as reminders of the trials he has faced and the countless hours spent perfecting his inventions.

## Backstory Hook

Milo Tinkerfoot was born into a loving halfling family in [[Glimmerhaven]]. His father was a skilled engineer, and his mother was a talented jeweler. They lived a peaceful life in a cozy home in the heart of the city. However, tragedy struck when a terrible accident occurred during one of his father's experimental inventions. The explosion not only destroyed their home but also claimed the lives of both his parents.

Milo and his older brother, [[Rindle]], were the sole survivors of the catastrophic event. Grief-stricken and traumatized, they clung to each other for support. Together, they vowed to carry on their parents' legacy, even as they grappled with their immense loss.

Their lives took a bleak turn when they set out on a journey to collect rare materials for their inventions. It was during this expedition that they fell prey to a merciless gang of bandits. These ruthless criminals ambushed the brothers in the dead of night, outnumbering them and overpowering their resistance.

Despite [[Rindle]]'s valiant efforts to protect Milo, the bandits were relentless. They captured [[Rindle]], leaving Milo beaten and bruised, watching helplessly as his beloved brother was dragged away into the darkness. The loss of his parents had been a devastating blow, but losing [[Rindle]] to the clutches of these heartless bandits was a pain that cut even deeper.

Milo, now alone and overwhelmed by grief, found solace in the most unlikely of places—the junkyards and dumpsters of [[Glimmerhaven]]. Among the discarded and forgotten relics of the city's past, he felt a strange connection to the broken and discarded pieces of machinery. The junkyards became his refuge, and he developed an uncanny knack for finding hidden treasures amidst the rubble.

One fateful day, as he sifted through a particularly cluttered dumpster, Milo's nimble fingers brushed against something cold and metallic. It was a partially broken pocket watch, its intricate gears and cogs peeking out from the debris. He carefully retrieved it, and despite its sorry state, Milo recognized the beauty within. He knew he had to restore it to its former glory.

Through days and nights of meticulous work, Milo poured his grief and determination into the watch's repair. It became a symbol of hope and resilience, a reminder of the inventive spirit that once thrived within his family. Little did he know that this pocket watch held a connection to another inventor, Gelbin Steambender.

[[Gelbin Steambender]], an eccentric gnome inventor of great renown, had owned the pocket watch. When he learned that Milo had not only found it but had also lovingly restored it to working order, he sought out the young halfling. Impressed by Milo's ingenuity and determination, Gelbin took him under his wing as a mentor and friend.

Under [[Gelbin Steambender|Gelbin]]'s tutelage, Milo honed his skills in engineering and invention. He learned to harness steam power, design intricate clockwork mechanisms, and even dabble in rudimentary magical applications.

Yet, amidst the joy of discovery and invention, a burning desire smoldered within Milo. He longed to avenge his brother [[Rindle]], to bring those merciless bandits to justice for tearing his family apart. He knew that his inventions could be harnessed to create a mechanical construct that would aid him in his quest for vengeance.

Milo Tinkerfoot's journey had transformed him from a grief-stricken halfling into a determined inventor and adventurer. With Gelbin's guidance and the memory of his family's love in his heart, he set out to build a mechanical companion that would help him track down those responsible for the loss of his beloved brother [[Rindle]].

## Motivations

Milo Tinkerfoot's belief system revolves around the pursuit of justice, the protection of his family's legacy, the power of innovation, and a deep-seated desire for vengeance.
