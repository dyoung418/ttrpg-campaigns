# Restov

> **Region**: Brevoy
> **Status**: Accessible

## Description

The southernmost city of Brevoy and the self-styled capital of the Aldori swordlords. A city-state in all but name — Restov chafes under Brevoy's rule and asserts its independence through its support of the Aldori tradition. [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]] hosts banquets here to cultivate useful adventurers.

The city's ambition to claim the [[Stolen Lands]] for settlement is what launched the campaign — the charter that sent the party south originated here.

## Connected Locations

- [[Stolen Lands]] — the territory to the south, the object of Restov's colonization effort
- [[Freehaven]] — the kingdom the party built with Restov's charter
