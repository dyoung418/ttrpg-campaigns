# Pitax

> **Region**: River Kingdoms (now part of Freehaven)
> **Type**: City — former rival kingdom capital
> **Status**: Accessible

## Description

The capital city of the former Pitax kingdom. Once ruled by the vain and antagonistic tyrant [[King Irovetti]], it was a rival power that aggressively opposed [[Freehaven]]. After the PCs overthrew Irovetti, Pitax joined Freehaven and is now one of its largest cities.

## Aspects

1. Former seat of a rival kingdom — cultural tensions may linger
2. Absorbed into Freehaven after Irovetti's defeat
3. Known to have been manipulated by [[Nyrissa]] — Irovetti was one of her pawns

## NPCs Found Here

- [[King Irovetti]] — former tyrant, now defeated

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> Per the campaign summary: Pitax was merely Nyrissa's pawn. Irovetti's madness was engineered by her to make Freehaven stronger and more worthy of being her final kingdom. The sword [[Briar]] was hidden in Irovetti's palace.
