# Embeth Forest

> **Region**: River Kingdoms
> **Type**: Wilderness — forest
> **Status**: Accessible (distant)

## Description

A wild forest in the River Kingdoms where [[Riven]] encountered [[The Few]] after leaving [[Kyonin]]. This is where he joined their cause as freedom fighters and where the group was based during the period before [[Rowan Half Dark]] corrupted their mission.

During Riven's three-month diplomatic mission to Kyonin, the delegation passed through the Embeth on the road south. [[Jameel Visser]] met them from the trees.

## The Ranger Hall

Jameel's base of operations: tucked between two rises beside a cold stream. No banners, no carved proclamation of importance. Sound roof, dry woodpile, paths swept clean. His scouts track movement through the forest before the movement knows it's being watched.

## The Embeth Agreement

An informal accord reached during the diplomatic mission: safe conduct for [[Freehaven]] caravans through the Embeth, open communication between Freehaven's rangers and Embeth's wardens, shared warning of threats moving between territories. Not a formal alliance — recognition. See also [[Freehaven-Kyonin Treaty]].

## NPCs Found Here

- [[Jameel Visser]] — ranger commander; runs the hall; old contact of Riven's
- [[The Few]] — Talia's band still operates in this region

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> - Embeth Forest is effectively The Few's home territory — and also Jameel's. If Talia encroaches, she'll run into his wardens.
> - If Talia is hunting Riven, agents from the Embeth Forest are the likely vector — but Jameel may be a counterweight.
> - The Embeth Agreement means Freehaven caravans have friends in the forest.
