# Embeth Forest

> **Region**: River Kingdoms
> **Type**: Wilderness — forest
> **Status**: Accessible (distant)

## Description

A wild forest in the River Kingdoms where [[Riven]] encountered [[The Few]] after leaving [[Kyonin]]. This is where he joined their cause as freedom fighters and where the group was based during the period before [[Rowan Half Dark]] corrupted their mission.

## NPCs Found Here

- [[The Few]] — Talia's band still operates in this region

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> - Embeth Forest is effectively The Few's home territory.
> - If Talia is hunting Riven, agents from the Embeth Forest are the likely vector.
