> **Region**: Stolen Lands

## Description

A bustling center of industry and craftsmanship built upon the ruins of an old fort, surrounded by rich quarries and dense forests. Known for its skilled stone masons, lumberyards, and thriving trade shops — a hub of productivity and innovation within [[Freehaven]].
