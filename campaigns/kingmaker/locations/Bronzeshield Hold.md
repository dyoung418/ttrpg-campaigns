# Bronzeshield Hold

> **Region**: Stolen Lands
> **Type**: Dwarven Hold / Settlement
> **Status**: Accessible

## Description

Built from the ancient remains of a dwarven stronghold. When the PCs first arrived, it had been overrun by trolls. After clearing the trolls, the PCs invited the exiled dwarven community to reclaim their ancestral home and join [[Freehaven]]. Today it stands as a thriving dwarven settlement within the kingdom.

## Aspects

1. Ancient dwarven architecture, partially restored
2. Close-knit exiled dwarven community returned to their homeland
3. Strong crafting and mining traditions

## NPCs Found Here

- [[Benedin]] — dwarf from this community; was a key travel companion of the PCs for an extended period

## Connected Locations

- [[Nightmarch]] — capital

## GM Notes

The dwarves were in exile before the PCs cleared the troll infestation and invited them back.
