# Tanglebriar

> **Region**: Kyonin (south)
> **Type**: Corrupted forest / demon-held territory
> **Status**: Hostile — held by Treerazer

## Description

A blighted, corrupted forest south of the elven homeland of [[Avennara|Kyonin]], occupied by the demon [[Treerazer]] and his undead forces. The elves repelled Treerazer from the heart of Kyonin but never fully drove him out. The Tanglebriar has festered in his grip ever since.

[[Riven]] fought Treerazer's undead here and in the surrounding areas for over fifty years before leaving Kyonin.

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> - Background lore for Riven. He uses the Tanglebriar as a concrete example of what happens when you let corruption take root: *"I've seen what happens when corruption is left to fester."*
> - Could become plot-relevant if news arrives from Kyonin.
