# Vesket

> **Region**: Stolen Lands — swamps and dense forests
> **Type**: Town
> **Status**: Accessible

## Description

A unique town that stands as a testament to the successful integration of lizardfolk and human communities, thriving amidst the swamps and dense forests of [[Freehaven]]. Its architecture blends seamlessly with the natural environment — sturdy, practical buildings that feel grown rather than built.

## Aspects

1. Dual lizardfolk/human heritage — a model of integration
2. Economy centered on lumber and boat trade; residents navigate waterways with skill
3. Deep respect for nature and the surrounding wilderness

## Notable Features

- Skilled waterway navigation and boat trade
- Architecture that merges with the natural environment
- Community guided by unity, cooperation, and respect for the wild
