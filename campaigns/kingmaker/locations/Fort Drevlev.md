# Fort Drevlev

> **Region**: Stolen Lands
> **Type**: Settlement / Fort
> **Status**: Accessible (now part of Freehaven)

## Description

A settlement originally founded by [[Hannis Drevlev]], one of the other adventurers who received a charter from [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]] alongside the PCs. Drevlev eventually turned treacherous, conspiring against [[Freehaven]] by allying with [[Pitax]] and the Tiger Lords. After the PCs defeated both Drevlev and his allies, Fort Drevlev was absorbed into Freehaven.

## Aspects

1. Founded by a former would-be peer of the PCs who turned villain
2. History of betrayal and treachery toward Freehaven
3. Now integrated into the kingdom

## NPCs Found Here

- [[Hannis Drevlev]] — founder, now defeated

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> The Tiger Lords were one of the factions Drevlev aligned with against Freehaven. Both the Tiger Lords and Drevlev were defeated before Fort Drevlev joined the kingdom.
