# Tuskwater River

> **Region**: Stolen Lands
> **Type**: River
> **Status**: Accessible

## Description

A river in the heart of the Stolen Lands. It borders the capital [[Nightmarch]] and holds, on an island in its middle, the [[Lizardfolk Village]] that became the town of [[Vesket]].

## Notable Features

- Borders [[Nightmarch]]
- Contains the island of the [[Lizardfolk Village]]
- North of [[Candlemere Island]]

## Connected Locations
