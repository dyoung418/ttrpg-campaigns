# Silverstep

> **Region**: Stolen Lands — lakeside, forested, mountainous
> **Type**: Town
> **Status**: Accessible

## Description

A serene and beautiful town nestled along the shores of Lake Silverstep, surrounded by dense woods and framed by majestic mountains. Known for its tranquil atmosphere and scholarly pursuits, it has become a haven for those seeking knowledge and peace within [[Freehaven]].

## Aspects

1. Natural beauty — lake, dense woods, mountain backdrop
2. Scholarly and contemplative culture; draws those who value wisdom
3. Quiet elegance; a place for reflection and learning

## Notable Features

- Sits on Lake Silverstep
- Center of learning and scholarship in the kingdom
- Very small — [[Pavel]]'s assessment of a "handful of tiny buildings" is accurate; he sarcastically called it "crowded"
- A knoll near the shore that [[Riven]] identified as a perfect painting spot — he imagined [[Lyriel]] setting her easel there

## Character Notes

- [[Riven]] said it's the most idyllic town in Freehaven and that it reminded him of [[Avennara]] where he grew up. He imagined [[Lyriel]] painting on the knoll and said, for the first time, that he might reach out to her.
- [[Linzi]] deliberately hasn't written a Freehaven Gazette article about Silverstep — she loves it too much and fears publicity would overrun it.

## The Temple of Desna

The bells of Desna echo across the lake and are audible from the meadow road. There is a temple to Desna at the edge of Silverstep; [[Cassandra]]'s Guard laid her body on its steps after the Battle of [[Willowshade]], stood bareheaded beside their weapons, and heard [[Riven]] speak. The city's funeral pyre rites are held on the lake — a boat sent out at night, no torches on shore, fire taking slow hold on the water.

## The Pitax War — The Battle of Silverstep

The [[Golden Talons]] under [[Lord Darian]] targeted Silverstep during the Pitax War. The battle unfolded in three phases:

1. **The Meadow Skirmish**: [[Cassandra]]'s twenty Guard held the hedgerow ditch against sixty of Darian's vanguard, inflicted casualties, and withdrew in order. Three Guard killed, five wounded.
2. **Council in the Dark**: [[Riven]] arrived with thirty Rangers (having sent [[Ekundayo]] north to [[Serenko]]). He and Cassandra planned the Willowshade trap in the grove overnight.
3. **The Battle of [[Willowshade]]**: Forty halberds feigned retreat across the ford into the valley hollow; Rangers on the ridges; Riven's druid storm turned the hollow into a killing ground. [[Lord Darian]] was killed by Riven's arrow. The Talons routed. Silverstep was saved.

**[[Cassandra]] died at Willowshade.** Her halberd was laid on the steps of Desna's temple. Her body was burned on a boat on the lake that night.
