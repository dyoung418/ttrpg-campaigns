# Silverstep

> **Region**: Stolen Lands — lakeside, forested, mountainous
> **Type**: Town
> **Status**: Accessible

## Description

A serene and beautiful town nestled along the shores of Lake Silverstep, surrounded by dense woods and framed by majestic mountains. Known for its tranquil atmosphere and scholarly pursuits, it has become a haven for those seeking knowledge and peace within [[Freehaven]].

## Aspects

1. Natural beauty — lake, dense woods, mountain backdrop
2. Scholarly and contemplative culture; draws those who value wisdom
3. Quiet elegance; a place for reflection and learning

## Notable Features

- Sits on Lake Silverstep
- Center of learning and scholarship in the kingdom
