# Avennara

> **Region**: Kyonin
> **Type**: City — ancient elven city
> **Status**: Accessible (distant from the Stolen Lands)

## Description

The ancient elven city within the lush, mystical forests of [[Kyonin]] where [[Riven]] was born and raised. Home to the elven scouts and their commanders. A place steeped in tradition, duty, and the sacred charge to protect the elven homeland from undead and demonic threats.

Riven left Avennara in shame after his court-martial, and has not returned.

## NPCs Found Here

- [[Aelir and Lytha]] — Riven's parents; deceased
- [[Faelir]] — Riven's druid mentor; presumably still here
- [[Lyriel]] — presumably still here, waiting for Riven's promised return

## GM Notes

- Avennara represents Riven's past — what he was, what he lost, and what he's afraid to face.
- A return to Avennara (or news arriving from it) could be a major emotional story beat.
