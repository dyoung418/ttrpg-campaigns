# The Rimebridge

The long stone span over the bottomless dark lake that separates [[Thousandbreaths]] from the [[House at the Edge of Time]] — the **only** crossing. The far shore flickers between mountain ranges and open starfield; the House itself oscillates beyond the water, a thousand carved faces watching from its doors.

[[Tarlaxian]], a gargantuan tarn linnorm, has claimed the lake as his *tarn* and guards the bridge as a wall. He drowns anything that tries to cross and **will not pursue into the House.** The span is sheathed in black ice from his freezing breath and the cold of the lake — hence the name.

## Aspects

- **Black-ice span** — **greater difficult terrain** (every 5 ft of movement costs an extra 10). A creature knocked prone or forcibly moved on the ice slides toward the nearest edge and must make a Reflex save (or Acrobatics) over the bottomless drop.
- **The bottomless freezing lake** — Tarlaxian's domain. He Submerges to become Unseen (denying ranged line of sight) and resurfaces where he chooses; anyone dragged off the edge faces a drowning + cold crisis (scary-but-recoverable).
- **The flickering far shore** — the House shifts between mountains and starfield; it literally stands at the edge of time. [[Briar|Briara]] grows *louder* the closer the party crosses, feeling the nearness of her origin, [[Nyrissa]].

The Rimebridge is the centerpiece fight of [[Session 103 - The Shape of Where I Used to Be]]. Full boss build and tactics: [[Tarlaxian]].
