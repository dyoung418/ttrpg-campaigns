# Lizardfolk Village

> **Region**: An island in the [[Tuskwater River]]
> **Type**: Village
> **Status**: Accessible — became the town of [[Vesket]]

## Description

A lizardfolk village set on an island in the middle of the [[Tuskwater River]], north of the will-o'-wisp-haunted [[Candlemere Island]]. Ruled by [[King Vesket]].

## History

In Session 13 the PCs came upon the village and were attacked on sight. When they saved [[King Vesket]] from a will-o'-wisp, he called a truce. The village eventually joined the kingdom of [[Freehaven]] and grew into the integrated lizardfolk/human town of [[Vesket]].

## Connected Locations

- [[Tuskwater River]] — the river the island sits in; also borders [[Nightmarch]]
- [[Candlemere Island]] — haunted island to the south
- [[Vesket]] — the town the village became
