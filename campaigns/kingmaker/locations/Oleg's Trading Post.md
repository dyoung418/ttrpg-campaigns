# Oleg's Trading Post

> **Region**: Rostland Hinterlands and Greenbelt
> **Type**: Frontier trading post
> **Status**: Accessible — grew into the town of [[Leveton]]

## Description

A small frontier trading post run by [[Oleg Leveton]] and his wife [[Svetlana Leveton]] — the PCs' first stop on entering the Stolen Lands (Session 5), and the seed from which the trade town of [[Leveton]] would later grow.

## History

When the PCs arrived, the post was being terrorized by bandits in service to [[The Stag Lord]], who came regularly to extort goods. The PCs took up Oleg and Svetlana's cause, tracked the bandits to their [[Thorn River Camp]], and broke them. It was here, too, that the PCs first met [[Ekundayo]].

The trade town of [[Leveton]] later grew from the post's foundations and carries the family name.

## Notable Features

- The first foothold of what became the kingdom of [[Freehaven]]
- Site where the party met [[Ekundayo]]
