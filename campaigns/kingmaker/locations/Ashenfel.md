# Ashenfel

> **Region**: Stolen Lands
> **Type**: Town — Centaur settlement
> **Status**: Accessible

## Description

A [[Nomen Centaurs|Nomen Centaur]] town. After the PCs rescued one of the Nomen ruler's daughters from the evil lich lord [[Vordakai]], the Nomen people agreed to become part of [[Freehaven]], and Ashenfel was integrated into the kingdom.

## Aspects

1. Centaur culture and traditions
2. Joined Freehaven through an act of rescue and goodwill
