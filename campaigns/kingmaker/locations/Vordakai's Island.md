# Vordakai's Island

> **Region**: Beyond the [[Valley of the Dead]], [[Nomen Heights]]
> **Type**: Tomb / lair (a tall pillar in a bay)
> **Status**: Cleared

## Description

A tall pillar of rock rising from the middle of a bay, beyond the [[Valley of the Dead]] — the entombment site and lair of the cyclops lich [[Vordakai]].

## History

Past the Valley of the Dead, the PCs found the island and entered it (Session 50). Within, they discovered [[Xamanthe]] held captive and freed her, then pressed on through horrors — including the dead of [[Varnhold]] and the undead ghost of [[Willas Gunderson]] — to confront and destroy Vordakai in his sepulchre. There they recovered [[Vordakai's Eye-Gem]] and freed the jarred souls of Varnhold's surviving residents.
