# Glimmerhaven

> **Region**: Unknown
> **Type**: City — halfling settlement
> **Status**: Accessible (distant from the Stolen Lands)

## Description

A city with a halfling community. [[Milo]]'s hometown. His parents — a skilled engineer and a talented jeweler — lived there until a catastrophic accident with one of his father's experimental inventions killed them both and destroyed their home.

After losing his brother [[Rindle]] to bandits on a later expedition, Milo found refuge in the city's junkyards and dumpsters, developing his skills among the discarded machinery. It was there he found the broken pocket watch that connected him to [[Gelbin Steambender]].

## NPCs Found Here

- [[Gelbin Steambender]] — gnome inventor; lived or operated in Glimmerhaven; now Milo's mentor

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> - Glimmerhaven is Milo's origin — the site of both his greatest losses and the beginning of his path as an inventor.
