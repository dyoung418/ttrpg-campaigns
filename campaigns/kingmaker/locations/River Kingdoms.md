# River Kingdoms

> **Region**: River Kingdoms
> **Status**: Accessible

## Description

A fractious collection of small, constantly-warring city-states and petty kingdoms in the southern Avistan frontier. Might makes right; borders shift with every season. [[Pitax]] is the most prominent kingdom in this region and the campaign's primary antagonist-state before [[King Irovetti]]'s defeat.

[[Freehaven]] itself is technically a River Kingdom — built from scratch in formerly unclaimed land.

## Connected Locations

- [[Stolen Lands]] — the sub-region where Freehaven was founded
- [[Pitax]] — the most powerful River Kingdom before its defeat and absorption
- [[Freehaven]] — the party's kingdom; one of the newer River Kingdoms

## Key NPCs

- [[King Irovetti]] — former ruler of Pitax; defeated by the party
