# Nomen Heights

> **Region**: Stolen Lands (east)
> **Type**: Region — wild steppes
> **Status**: Accessible; Nomen Centaur territory

## Description

Wild, open steppes stretching toward the mountains. The grass runs long and the wind cuts across the plain. The [[Tors of Levenies]] loom in the distance to the east, frequently lit by afternoon thunderstorms. This is the homeland of the [[Nomen Centaurs]].

## Aspects

1. Open steppe country — visibility is long, cover is scarce
2. Centaur patrols move through regularly; travelers who enter without escort are viewed with suspicion
3. The land has a spacious, melancholy quality — easy to feel the weight of one's thoughts here

## NPCs Found Here

- [[Aecora]] — leader of the Nomen Centaurs; based at [[Ashenfel]]

## Connected Locations

- [[Tors of Levenies]] — mountain formation to the east
- [[Ashenfel]] — Nomen Centaur town; centaur community within Freehaven
- [[Varnhold]] — the ghost town discovered on the edge of this territory

## GM Notes

> - The centaur escorts' expressions shifted from stern disapproval to thoughtful appreciation as they overheard the party's conversation — centaurs have finer hearing than the party realized.
