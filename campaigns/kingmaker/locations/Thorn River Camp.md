# Thorn River Camp

> **Region**: Rostland Hinterlands and Greenbelt
> **Type**: Bandit camp
> **Status**: Cleared

## Description

The camp of the bandits who terrorized [[Oleg's Trading Post]] on behalf of [[The Stag Lord]].

## History

After [[Oleg Leveton]] and [[Svetlana Leveton]] told the PCs of the bandits extorting their post, the PCs tracked the raiders to the Thorn River Camp and battled them (Session 5). In that fight they met [[Cassandra]], a bandit who clearly wished to turn from her old life and break with the Stag Lord.
