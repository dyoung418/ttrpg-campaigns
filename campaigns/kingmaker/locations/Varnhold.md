# Varnhold

> **Region**: Stolen Lands
> **Type**: Town (part of Freehaven)
> **Status**: Accessible — recovered and integrated into Freehaven

## Description

A town with a haunted past. Originally founded by [[Maegar Varn]], a good-hearted adventurer who received a charter from [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]] alongside the PCs. Varnhold grew into a thriving settlement — until [[Vordakai]] descended upon it.

The lich, freshly reawakened from centuries of sleep, pulled the souls of every resident into his lair to harvest their memories and knowledge of the modern world. The town was left a perfect ghost town — empty, untouched, as if everyone had simply vanished in the middle of their lives.

## History

The PCs discovered and solved the mystery of Varnhold's disappearance by locating [[Vordakai]]'s lair. They freed what souls they could, but [[Maegar Varn]] himself was killed by Vordakai and could not be saved. The surviving townspeople, having no leader to return to, asked to join [[Freehaven|Nightmarch]].

## Aspects

1. A town that survived a literal soul-harvest by an ancient lich
2. Founded by a charter-holder peer of the PCs — good man, tragic end
3. Its residents carry the trauma of having been pulled from their lives by Vordakai

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> Varnhold is one of the campaign's most striking set-pieces — an entire living town emptied overnight, every meal still on the table, every door still open. The mystery of what happened here was a major story beat.
