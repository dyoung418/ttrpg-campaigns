# Valley of the Dead

> **Region**: [[Nomen Heights]]
> **Type**: Forbidden valley
> **Status**: Accessible (taboo to the Nomen)

## Description

A valley in the [[Nomen Heights]] that the [[Nomen Centaurs]] hold under strict taboo. Beyond it lies [[Vordakai's Island]].

## History

[[Xamanthe]], daughter of [[Aecora]], broke the Nomen taboo by entering the valley after glimpsing mysterious shapes there, and went missing for days — drawing the PCs into the search (Session 40). Pressing past the valley, the PCs reached [[Vordakai's Island]], where Xamanthe was being held captive by the lich [[Vordakai]] (Session 50).
