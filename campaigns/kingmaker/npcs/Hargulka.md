## Personality

A tyrant and warlord. [[Riven]] and [[Pavel]] both cite him alongside [[The Stag Lord]] as an example of the kind of destructive leader they do not want to be. Pavel's reassurance to Riven: *"You're no Stag Lord, and you're certainly no Hargulka."*

## Background

Hargulka led the troll clan that had overrun [[Bronzeshield Hold]], the ancient dwarven stronghold, driving the dwarven community into exile. The PCs cleared the trolls, defeated Hargulka, and invited the exiled dwarves to reclaim their ancestral home — which they did, founding the current settlement of Bronzeshield Hold as part of [[Freehaven]].
