# Kargstaad

**Creature 19** | Unique, CE, Large, Cold, Giant, Humanoid (Variant frost giant)

> [!info] GM decision (2026-07-02): encounter skipped
> The party is bypassing the Frozen Boneyard (Glade C) — Kargstaad will not be run. He remains in his glade as set dressing/lore; keep the stat block in case plans change.

A foul-tempered four-armed frost giant who serves as anchor and guardian of **the Frozen Boneyard (Glade C)** in [[Thousandbreaths]]. He was "collected" by Nyrissa as a trophy ages ago and has lived in this frozen mock-cemetery ever since, eternally provided for with food and water.

He commands 6 Kargstaad's Giants (CR 15) — when the Frozen Bloom manifests on Day 16, he sends them through first. By the time the PCs arrive at Glade C, his giants have already been defeated (or are still rampaging elsewhere).

## Stats

- **AC** 43 | **HP** 360, fast healing 20
- **Fort** +35, **Ref** +31, **Will** +33; juggernaut (successful Fort → crit success)
- **Immunities**: cold | **Weaknesses**: fire 20
- **Perception** +33; low-light vision
- **Languages**: Aklo, Common, Jotun, Sylvan
- **Skills**: Athletics +39, Crafting +33, Intimidation +37, Stealth +33 (+37 in snow)

**Items**: +2 greater resilient hide armor, +2 striking hatchets ×4, sack with 5 rocks

## Key Abilities

- **Rage** (1 action): +25 temp HP, +12 damage to thrown rocks, +6 damage to hatchets. −1 AC, can't use concentrate actions.
- **Hatchet Onslaught** (3 actions): Stride half speed, then 4 hatchet Strikes (MAP applies normally). Requires all 4 hands to hold hatchets.
- **Chill Breath** (1 action, cold): 9d6 cold in cone, DC 41 Reflex.
- **Ice Stride**: Ignore movement penalties from ice/snow.

## Tactics

Kargstaad rages and charges into melee immediately. If he can't reach the PCs, hurls gravestones as improvised rocks. He fights to the death — the arrival of the PCs gives him a chance to redeem his giants' failure by capturing them for Nyrissa.
