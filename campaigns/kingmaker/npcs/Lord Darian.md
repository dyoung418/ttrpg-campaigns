# Lord Darian

> **Role**: Commander — [[Golden Talons]] vanguard
> **Location**: Unknown
> **Faction**: [[Pitax]] (formerly)
> **Status**: Unknown — fate at Silverstep unresolved

## Description

Mounted, white-plumed, armor polished. He rode at a walk while his men fought at a march — the deliberate pace of a man who expects the field to organize itself around him. He surveyed [[Cassandra]]'s Guard "as though they were an inconvenience on his road."

## Personality

Contemptuous and cold. When the Guard's volleys thinned his line and her halberdiers dragged men into the ditch, he didn't respond with anger or aggression — he raised his sword, reined in, and let the numbers do the work. He didn't chase the retreating Guard. He didn't need to. His reinforcements were days away; Silverstep wasn't going anywhere. The white plume, the calm seat on his horse, the banner planted in the churned grass — all of it deliberate theater, a message to the defenders: *you are already losing.*

## Background

Led the sixty-man vanguard of the [[Golden Talons]] to the meadow outside [[Silverstep]] during the Pitax War. Met [[Cassandra]]'s Guard in a skirmish at the hedgerow ditch: she had twenty halberdiers, he had sixty mercenaries with locked shields and forward spears. He inflicted three killed and five wounded, took a handful of casualties, and held the field after she withdrew.

That night in his pavilion he called Cassandra "a thief, a bandit in the service of the Stag Lord," and dismissed the scarred captain's assessment that she'd held longer than expected: *"So would a wolf in a trap."* He raised his goblet and swore not to drink again until it held Silverstep wine in the city's hall. He carried the empty goblet at his belt at dawn — a gesture of theater and certainty.

He never drank from it. He walked his men into the [[Willowshade]] trap and was killed by one of [[Riven]]'s flaming arrows through the breastplate as he rose in his stirrups to rally the breaking line. He fell soundlessly, plume crushed in the mud, and the goblet-banner sagged with him.

## Relationships

- [[Cassandra]] — opponent; he dismissed her entirely; she used that dismissal to kill him
