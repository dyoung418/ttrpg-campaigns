# King Vesket

> **Role**: King of the lizardfolk of the [[Lizardfolk Village]]
> **Location**: [[Vesket]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

The lizardfolk king who ruled the island village in the middle of the [[Tuskwater River]]. Proud and initially hostile, he became a steadfast ally of [[Freehaven]] after the PCs saved his life.

## Background

In Session 13 the PCs came upon the [[Lizardfolk Village]], north of the haunted [[Candlemere Island]]. The lizardfolk attacked on sight, but when the PCs saved King Vesket from a will-o'-wisp, he called a truce. His village eventually joined the kingdom of [[Freehaven]] and grew into the town of [[Vesket]], which bears his name.

## Relationships

- [[Freehaven]] — his people joined the kingdom after the truce
- [[Vesket]] — the integrated lizardfolk/human town named for him
