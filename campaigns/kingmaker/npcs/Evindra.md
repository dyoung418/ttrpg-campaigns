# Evindra

> **Role**: Nereid; fey ally and informant
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A nereid — a water fey. Her appearance is not recorded; fill in as portrayed at the table.

## Background

Evindra knows the full story of [[Nyrissa]]'s fall from grace: how she tried to elevate herself to the status of the Eldest, how the Lantern King and the other Eldest punished her by tearing out her capacity for love and forging it into the sword [[Briar]], and how Nyrissa has been systematically destroying kingdoms ever since in hopes of completing The Apology and recovering what was taken from her.

She also understands the significance of [[Briar]] — that it holds Nyrissa's stolen emotions, that it grows more powerful the more it is exposed to First World enemies, and that Nyrissa desperately wants it back.

She knows the location of the entrance to [[Thousandbreaths]] — the Castle of Knives portal in the Thousand Voices forest — and was the one who pointed the PCs toward it.

## How She Came to Help

The PCs befriended Evindra and gained her trust. In return, she warned them that First World forces were about to assault the Stolen Lands, and shared everything she knew about Nyrissa, Briar, and the path into Thousandbreaths. She is an ally, not a neutral party — she wants Nyrissa stopped.

## What She Told the PCs

- [[Nyrissa]]'s full backstory: the hubris, the Eldest's punishment, the Apology, the 1,000 kingdoms
- The nature of [[Briar]]: what it holds, why Nyrissa wants it, what it can do
- The location of the entrance to [[Thousandbreaths]]: the Castle of Knives portal in the Thousand Voices forest
- That First World forces would assault the Stolen Lands (the blooms of [[The Month of Destruction]])

## Relationships

- [[Nyrissa]] — knows her history; opposed to her; this may stem from personal history or principle
- [[Briar]] — understands the sword's nature and significance
- [[Thousandbreaths]] — knows its entrance and, presumably, something of what lies within

## GM Notes

- Her motivations for helping the PCs are worth defining: does she have personal history with Nyrissa? Is she a servant of the Lantern King passing on intelligence? Or simply a fey who opposes what Nyrissa has become?
- She could resurface at the House or as a post-campaign contact if the forgiveness path is taken.
