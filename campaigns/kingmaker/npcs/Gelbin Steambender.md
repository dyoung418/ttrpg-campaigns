# Gelbin Steambender

> **Role**: Gnome inventor; Milo's mentor and friend
> **Location**: [[Glimmerhaven]] (presumably)
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

An eccentric gnome inventor of great renown. Owns a pocket watch of intricate craftsmanship that ended up lost and broken in a [[Glimmerhaven]] junkyard — where [[Milo]] found it and lovingly restored it to working order.

## Personality

Eccentric. Impressed by ingenuity and determination. When he learned Milo had not only found his pocket watch but restored it, he sought the young halfling out and took him under his wing.

## Background

Gelbin took [[Milo]] on as a mentee after the pocket watch reunion. Under his tutelage, Milo learned to harness steam power, design clockwork mechanisms, and apply rudimentary magic to his work. He is the reason Milo became a capable Inventor rather than just a talented junkyard scavenger.

## Relationships

- [[Milo]] — his student; found and restored his lost pocket watch; Gelbin shaped Milo's technical foundation
- [[Glimmerhaven]] — associated with this city
