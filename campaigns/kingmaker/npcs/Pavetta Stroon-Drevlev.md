# Pavetta Stroon-Drevlev

> **Role**: Baroness; wife of [[Hannis Drevlev]]
> **Location**: [[Restov]] (sent there by the PCs)
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

The Baroness of [[Fort Drevlev]] — a person of very powerful will and determination, sister to the magician [[Imeckus Stroon]] and wife to the treacherous [[Hannis Drevlev]].

## Background

When the PCs took Drevlev Keep and brought down Hannis Drevlev (Session 60), they encountered his wife, Baroness Pavetta. Rather than an enemy, they found a woman of formidable will who gave them counsel and impressed them deeply. They were impressed enough to send her on to [[Restov]] rather than treat her as a captive.

## Relationships

- [[Hannis Drevlev]] — her husband (defeated by the PCs)
- [[Imeckus Stroon]] — her brother
- [[Restov]] — where the PCs sent her

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> A person of powerful will, free of her husband's legacy and now in Restov — a potential future player on the regional stage.
