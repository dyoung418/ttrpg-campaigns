# Willas Gunderson

> **Role**: Treasure hunter
> **Location**: [[Varnhold]] / [[Vordakai's Island]]
> **Status**: Deceased — encountered later as an undead ghost

## Description

A treasure hunter from [[Varnhold]] whose fatal mistranslation of an ancient map set the Varnhold Vanishing in motion.

## Background

Following an ancient map, Willas mistranslated it: what he read as a region of vast magical *wealth* was in fact a warning of vast magical *danger* — the entombment site of the cyclops lich [[Vordakai]]. Willas traveled there and triggered the sequence that woke Vordakai from his thousand-year slumber (Session 40). Realizing how long he had slept, Vordakai set about restoring his power, beginning by abducting the entire town of Varnhold to harvest its knowledge.

The PCs later encountered the undead ghost of Willas Gunderson within Vordakai's lair on [[Vordakai's Island]] (Session 50).

## Relationships

- [[Vordakai]] — the lich he unwittingly awakened
- [[Varnhold]] — his home town, abducted as a consequence of his blunder
