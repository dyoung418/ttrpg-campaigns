# The Oaktop Ankou

> **Role**: The First World general behind the disciplined counter-ambush at the [[Oaktop]] silver mine
> **Status**: Defeated — revealed at the climax of [[RP 25.1 — Pavel's Confusion]] and killed in the fight that followed (that RP source cuts off on Pavel's charge; the party went on to destroy it)
> **Faction**: [[Nyrissa]]'s bloom forces (see [[The Month of Destruction]])

## Summary

An **Ankou** — a fey nightmare from the First World — that commanded [[Nyrissa]]'s garrison occupying the [[Oaktop]] silver mine. It was the intelligence behind the garrison's surprising **discipline**: when the party's feint-and-bait ambush should have scattered a mob of redcaps, trolls, and satyrs, the enemy instead pivoted with drilled precision into a **counter-ambush** — pre-cranked crossbow volleys, coordinated troll bodyguards screening spellcasting shamans, and lightning called down on the party's hidden archers. Only once its minions were being put to flight did the general emerge from the shadows to rally them and test its own might against the weakened Nightmarch leaders.

## Appearance

A gargantuan figure — roughly **thirty feet tall** — of night and flame:
- Black wings spread behind it like a velvety cloak, held motionless (it hovers without beating them).
- A huge ribbed chest of black bone with **orange flame leaking between the ribs** and from its eye sockets.
- Monstrous arms ending in razor-tipped claws; a dragon-like snout.
- No legs — its lower body is a single **spiked, dangling tail**.

## What Happened (RP 25.1)

The Ankou revealed itself as the Oaktop battle turned, its minions cheering as the darkness pulled together into its form. [[Pavel]] — freshly revived by [[Great Aunt Sumi]] after a troll shaman's lightning dropped him — recognized it for what it was and, finding grim clarity about his own fears, hoisted [[Briar]] and **charged its flaming head**. The RP source cuts off on that charge, but the party went on to **kill the Ankou** and break the Oaktop garrison.
