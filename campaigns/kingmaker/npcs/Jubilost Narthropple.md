# Jubilost Narthropple

> **Role**: Gnome explorer and cartographer
> **Status**: Alive — joined the kingdom
> **Location**: [[Freehaven]]

## Description

The sharp-tongued leader of a nine-gnome expedition attempting to map the Stolen Lands.

## Background

During the kingdom's expansion (Session 25), the PCs came upon Jubilost's expedition as they tried to cross a river by wagon and were about to be swept over a waterfall. The PCs saved them. Grateful, Jubilost joined the kingdom of [[Freehaven]], lending his cartographic skills.

## Relationships

- [[Freehaven]] — the kingdom he joined after his rescue
