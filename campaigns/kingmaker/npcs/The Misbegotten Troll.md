# The Misbegotten Troll

> **Role**: Normal anchor of Glade I — The Baleful Lantern; during Chapter 10 the anchor transfers to the building itself
> **CR**: 18
> **Status**: Alive
> **Parent**: [[Thousandbreaths]]

## Summary

Serves as the normal tether of the Baleful Lantern glade — a beehive-shaped structure lit by decapitated human heads used as lanterns. During Chapter 10 of [[Chapter 10 - Sound of a Thousand Screams|the bloom chapter]], the anchor transfers to the building itself, which the party must collapse to break the glade.

## Tactics

When attacked from over 100 feet away, retreats into the Lantern to regroup. Directs the 8× Black Smilodons to attack archers in the back first.
