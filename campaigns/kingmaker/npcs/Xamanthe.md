## Personality

Dignified and prideful — her first instinct at Riven's race challenge was to note what she *would have said* a month ago (something pointed about two-leggers), but she checked herself out of gratitude. Her humor surfaces as she recovers: she offered to enter the party in the **kinderfoal division** of the spring festival as a "fair" race. Genuinely funny, deliberately at their expense.

Deep loyalty to her herd. Shaken by her captivity under [[Vordakai]] — he was worse than even centaur legends described — but the glimpses of her true personality are beginning to show through the fear.

## Background

Daughter of [[Aecora]], the leader of the [[Nomen Centaurs]]. Was captured by [[Vordakai]] and held in his lair, where a curse was placed on her legs. She was rescued by the party after they defeated Vordakai. A Nomen enchanter subsequently removed the curse; her legs were slowly regaining movement in the aftermath.

She explicitly acknowledged a debt to the party: *"I will never forget your kindness. Somehow I will pay my debt to you."* She notes that humans have generally not been good to centaur kind — her gratitude is genuine but given against that backdrop.

She expressed concern for her herd after the encounter: Vordakai was far worse than centaur legends told, and she wonders what brought him back after 100,000 years and how her people will survive such a threat. She takes comfort in her mother being a strong leader, but the scale of what she faced is beyond anything their traditions prepared them for.

## Relationships

- [[Aecora]] — her mother; leader of the Nomen Centaurs; Xamanthe takes some comfort in her strength
- [[Riven]] — freed her; she has pledged to repay the debt; he invited her to a race (she countered with the kinderfoal division)
- [[Vordakai]] — her captor; the experience has left her shaken
