# Saint Flint

*(Personal name unknown — Saint Flint is her title. [[Eri Malai]] is her student who borrowed the name in her absence.)*

> **Role**: Leader of the [[Nagaji Monastic Order]]
> **Location**: [[Pitax]] (returned after Eri Malai cast Guiding Star)
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A stern-looking nagaji woman. She walks into a room — a church mid-sermon, no less — with complete authority and no apparent awareness that interrupting is unusual. She takes her staff back, uses it to bop her student on the head, and delivers a multi-count rebuke without pausing for his responses.

## Personality

Formidable and precise. She had a plan for her disappearance, she executed it, and she is irritated that the plan required no explanation because it was obvious to her and apparently not to [[Eri Malai]]. She does not shout. She does not need to. Her rebukes land because they are accurate: *"Did you really think you could pass for me? I'm certain you've fooled no one."*

Underneath the exasperation is evident care — she called him "one of her favorite idiot students" (via [[Engelidis]]'s report), and she waited in the woods for days rather than simply leaving when [[Eri Malai]] failed to come find her. She expected him to use the Guiding Star correctly. He didn't. She waited anyway.

## Background

The leader of the [[Nagaji Monastic Order]], which operated inside [[Pitax]] during [[King Irovetti]]'s rule. Irovetti grew uneasy about her growing influence, and [[Engelidis]] — who enjoyed their tea conversations — inadvertently mentioned enough to put her at risk. She made a calculated decision: disappear so that Irovetti would not take reprisals against her people. She left her staff and vestments with [[Eri Malai]] (her "favorite idiot student") and no note, trusting him to understand that the staff's Guiding Star property could recall her when the time was right.

He didn't cast the spell. He wore her robes and her name for the duration of the war instead.

She learned Irovetti was dead from a traveling merchant. She came out of the woods and walked back into Pitax herself.

## Relationships

- [[Eri Malai]] — her student; borrowed her name, robes, and staff; she's exasperated and fond of him in roughly equal measure
- [[Engelidis]] — the naga who used to take tea with her in the palace; inadvertently spilled enough to Irovetti to force her hand; she doesn't appear to hold a grudge
- [[The Starlight Staff]] — her staff; enchanted with Guiding Star; left behind intentionally as a recall mechanism; reclaimed at the reunion

## Session History

- Pitax War period: in hiding; waiting for someone to cast Guiding Star; heard nothing; learned of Irovetti's death from a merchant instead
- Post-war reunion: walked into Eri Malai's church service; reclaimed her staff; delivered her rebuke; restored to her role
