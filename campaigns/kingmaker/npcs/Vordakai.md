## Background

An ancient cyclops lich who had slept for centuries before reawakening. His return triggered a localized zombie apocalypse in the Stolen Lands. Freshly awake and seeking to fill in the gaps of centuries of missed history, Vordakai descended on [[Varnhold]] and harvested every soul in the town — including its founder [[Maegar Varn]] — to extract their memories of the modern world. The town was left a perfect ghost town.

He also captured the daughter of the [[Nomen Centaurs|Nomen Centaur]] ruler. The PCs tracked down his lair, freed what souls from Varnhold they could (though Maegar Varn was already dead), and rescued the Nomen ruler's daughter. This earned the Nomens' loyalty and led to [[Ashenfel]] joining [[Freehaven]]. The surviving people of Varnhold also joined Freehaven.
