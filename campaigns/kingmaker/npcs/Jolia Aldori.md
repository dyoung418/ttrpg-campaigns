# Edina's Sister

> **Role**: [[Edina Aldori]]'s sister; niece of [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]]
> **Status**: Deceased — killed during the hunting competition

## Background

One of two Aldori sisters (nieces of [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]]) who participated in the same hunting competition as the party. When the party came upon the sisters, they were in serious trouble with a stronger-than-expected beast. The party joined the fight. The beast was defeated. She died from her wounds before it was over.

[[Pavel]] was present throughout.
