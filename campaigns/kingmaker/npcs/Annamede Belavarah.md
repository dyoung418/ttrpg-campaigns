# Annamede Belavarah

> **Role**: Bard of [[Pitax]]
> **Location**: [[Pitax]]
> **Status**: Alive — ally of the PCs

## Description

A bard from [[Pitax]] who is no admirer of [[King Irovetti]].

## Background

The PCs first met Annamede at the Hunting Competition thrown by the Embeth Travelers (Session 30). Later, when the PCs infiltrated [[Pitax]] to bring down Irovetti (Session 80), she was among the contacts who proved helpful — part of the network that became the underground Fox Alliance.

## Relationships

- [[King Irovetti]] — the tyrant of Pitax she opposed
- [[Gaspere Liacenza]] — fellow Pitaxian contact in the Fox Alliance
- [[Jameel Visser]] — both attended the Hunting Competition
