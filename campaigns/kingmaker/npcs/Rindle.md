## Background

Milo's older brother. When the two of them set out on an expedition to collect rare materials for their inventions, they were ambushed by bandits. Despite fighting to protect [[Milo]], Rindle was captured and dragged away into the darkness. Milo was left beaten and bruised, unable to stop it.

His fate is unknown. Milo has never stopped wanting to find him.

## The Construct

[[Milo]] built a mechanical construct and named it Rindle — both in his brother's honor and as the mechanical companion that will help him track down the bandits responsible. The construct "Rindle" that travels with Milo and the party is this machine.
