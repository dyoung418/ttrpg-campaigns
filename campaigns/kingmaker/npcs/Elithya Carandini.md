# Elithya Carandini

> **Role**: Mother of the [[Carandini Family]]
> **Faction**: [[Carandini Family]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A woodland elf; mother to the nineteen Carandini children alongside her husband [[Erevan Carandini]].

## Personality

*Unestablished — offscreen backstory figure.* Together with [[Erevan Carandini]], stubborn enough to name all nineteen children in strict alphabetical sequence and see it through.

## Background

With [[Erevan Carandini]], parented the nineteen Carandini children and established the family's naming convention — each child named for the next consecutive letter of the alphabet plus "-ee" (Aee, Bee, [[Cee]], Dee, and so on). [[Lee]] is their twelfth child.

- [[Cee]] — third-born; the family's Court Arcanist
