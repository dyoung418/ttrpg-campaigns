# The Nightmare Rook

> **Role**: Anchor of Glade D — The Nightmare Spire
> **CR**: 20 (gargantuan bird)
> **Status**: Alive
> **Parent**: [[Thousandbreaths]]

## Summary

A gargantuan bird perched on a 200-foot stone spire (appears mile-tall) in a mile-wide clearing of jagged hills and thorny plains. Primary tactic: **Snatch** PCs and drop them into other glades (the Mire or the Fruiting Orchard). Carries passengers only at [[Nyrissa]]'s request.

## Tactics

At <90 HP, lies about ferrying PCs to safety — drops them in the Mire or the Orchard instead. *Bloom trophy*: black raven feathers (linked to the Nights of Dread bloom).
