# Tovan

> **Role**: Veteran second — [[Riven]]'s Rangers
> **Location**: [[Freehaven]], field operations
> **Faction**: [[Freehaven]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

Scarred face, short sword, strip of cloth always ready to clean the blade. Moves with the efficient economy of a man who has been doing this for a very long time and has no energy to waste on appearances.

## Personality

Dry humor that surfaces exactly when things are at their bleakest. He is the counterweight to Riven's relentless forward momentum — not because he disagrees, but because someone has to say it. When Riven announced the Rangers needed to march immediately after a battle, Tovan said: *"Always the hard road."* He matched Riven's pace without complaint.

## Background

A scarred veteran who serves as [[Riven]]'s second among the Rangers during the [[Pitax]] War. He knows his commander well enough to pitch for a rest when the men need it and to fall in line the moment he hears the reason. After the road ambush, when [[Riven]] said the [[Golden Talons]] outnumber [[Cassandra]] two to one, Tovan shook his head — and kept walking.

## Relationships

- [[Riven]] — commander; Tovan reads him well and serves as a grounded foil to his urgency

## Session History

- First appeared: Pitax War RP thread (road ambush — with Riven, post-ambush march)
