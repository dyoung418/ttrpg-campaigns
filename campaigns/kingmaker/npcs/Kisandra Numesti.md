# Kisandra Numesti

> **Role**: [[Ekundayo]]'s fiancée; daughter of [[Terrion Numesti]]
> **Location**: [[Nightmarch]] / [[Fort Drevlev]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

Daughter of [[Terrion Numesti]] and fiancée of [[Ekundayo]]. The one who carried warning of the alliance against [[Freehaven]] out of [[Fort Drevlev]].

## Background

[[Ekundayo]] settled in [[Nightmarch]] after the Stag Lord battle and became engaged to Kisandra.

When [[Pitax]], [[Hannis Drevlev]] and the [[Tiger Lords]] formed their alliance against [[Freehaven]], the Tiger Lords demanded hostages from Drevlev. Drevlev ordered his general [[Terrion Numesti]] — Kisandra's father — to give up one of his daughters. Terrion refused and was imprisoned, and Kisandra's **sister** was taken forcibly by the Tiger Lords. Kisandra fled and travelled hard from [[Fort Drevlev]] to [[Bronzeshield Hold]], arriving to warn the PCs that an army of trolls and Pitaxian mercenaries was marching on the Hold, and to beg help for her father and sister (Session 60).

The PCs broke the army at Bronzeshield Hold, freed Terrion at Fort Drevlev, and later rescued her captured sister from [[Armag Reborn]] and the Tiger Lords (Session 65).

## Relationships

- [[Ekundayo]] — her fiancé; settled in Nightmarch
- [[Terrion Numesti]] — her father; imprisoned by Drevlev for refusing to surrender a daughter
- [[Hannis Drevlev]] — whose treachery endangered her family
- [[Tiger Lords]] — took her sister captive
