# Melianse

> **Role**: Pixie; protector of her stretch of forest
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A pixie of the Stolen Lands who fiercely opposed the felling of her trees.

## Background

During the kingdom's expansion (Session 25), the PCs were called to settle a dispute between a crew of loggers and Melianse, who did not want her trees chopped down. The PCs mediated the conflict.
