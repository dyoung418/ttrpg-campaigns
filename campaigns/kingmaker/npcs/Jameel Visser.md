# Jameel Visser

> **Role**: Ranger commander of the Embeth Forest wardens
> **Location**: [[Embeth Forest]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A broad-shouldered man in a weather-dark cloak. Beard shorter than Riven remembered, face more lined, eyes just as sharp. Speaks plainly and without ceremony — to Riven and to anyone else.

## Background

An old contact of [[Riven]]'s from his years before Freehaven. Commands the wardens who operate out of a ranger hall in the [[Embeth Forest]]: tucked between two rises beside a cold stream, no banners, no carved proclamation of importance, sound roof, dry woodpile, paths swept clean.

When Riven's diplomatic delegation passed through the Embeth on the road to [[Kyonin]], Jameel's foresters stepped from the trees — bows unstrung but near. The reunion was brief and unsentimental: a clasped forearm, a comment about the delegation's pace, and [[Linzi]] earning her own assessment within the hour.

Over venison stew and maps that evening, they talked of trade paths, fordable crossings, monster sign, raiders, and border custom. Riven handled anything involving terrain and field craft. Linzi handled the rest with such precision that one of Jameel's captains began watching her the way men watch drawn steel. By dusk, nothing had been signed, but something had shifted: the [[Freehaven-Kyonin Treaty|Embeth Agreement]] — safe conduct for Freehaven caravans, open communication between ranger companies, shared warning of threats. Not a formal alliance. Recognition. Freehaven would not be treated as a camp that had grown too large for its own good.

At the end of the evening, Jameel walked outside with Riven under the pines and said what he'd been working toward: *"I thought you'd grow harder if you lived long enough. Meaner, maybe."* When Riven said he'd tried that, Jameel replied simply: *"Aye. You did. Now you look like you know what you're for."*

His final word on Linzi: *"Keep her close, Riven. She makes you almost approachable."*

## Relationships

- [[Riven]] — old contact; mutual respect between foresters; speaks to him as one ranger to another, not as to a king
- [[Linzi]] — immediately impressed; *"Keep her close. She makes you almost approachable."*
- [[Embeth Forest]] — his territory; his ranger hall is here
