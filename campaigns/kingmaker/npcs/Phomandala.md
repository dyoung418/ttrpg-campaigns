# Phomandala

**Creature 19** | Unique, LE, Medium, Dragon, Humanoid (Variant female medusa, primal sorcerer)

A medusa whom [[Nyrissa]] infused with jabberwock blood in an early experiment to create lesser jabberwocks. Only Phomandala survived, transformed — she gained a pair of large cobras amid her serpentine hair, and over centuries of living in the First World, she developed sorcerer powers. Her bloodline is literally jabberwock-derived (functions as draconic bloodline, but sonic energy line instead of an element, primal spell list).

She has lived in the **Ruined Halls (M3)** of the [[House at the Edge of Time]] for a long time, in a self-imposed "retirement," spending centuries debating the role of law vs. chaos in the First World with two whimwyrms. Neither side has budged. They barely noticed the bloom events.

## Stats

- **AC** 45 (all-around vision) | **HP** 300
- **Fort** +33, **Ref** +35, **Will** +32
- **Perception** +32; darkvision
- **Languages**: Aklo, Common, Draconic, Sylvan
- **Skills**: Acrobatics +35, Arcana +34, Deception +37, Intimidation +37, First World Lore +37, Nature +35, Religion +33
- **Petrifying Gaze** (aura): 30 feet, DC 38

**Key Spells** (spontaneous, DC 41): 10th *revival* (1 slot); 9th *chain lightning*, *disjunction*, *massacre*, *overwhelming presence*, *heal*; 8th *horrid wilting*, *prismatic wall*; 7th *eclipse burst*, *finger of death*, *regenerate*, *wall of thorns*; 6th *baleful polymorph*, *dragon form*, *stone to flesh*, *true seeing*

**Sorcerer Bloodline (Jabberwock)**: *dragon breath* (sonic line), *dragon claws*, *dragon wings* — 3 focus points.

**Quickened Casting** (1/day): Reduce cast time of ≤7th-level spell by 1 action.

**Woodland Stride**: Ignores all plant-based difficult terrain (this room is all plants).

## Tactics

**On PCs arriving**: She doesn't attack immediately. She poses a philosophical question: *"What is the proper role of law and chaos in the First World?"*

- PCs favor **law** → Phomandala smirks; whimwyrms attack in anger (Round 1).
- PCs favor **chaos** → Phomandala shrieks and attacks; whimwyrms join Round 2.
- PCs argue **both are equal** → Everyone attacks Round 1.

**In combat**:
- Stays out of melee, moves through the ruined halls' difficult terrain freely with Woodland Stride.
- Uses **Focus Gaze** and spells against ranged targets.
- When both whimwyrms are slain: casts *revival* to bring them back.
- Fights to the death (all three are frustrated about the interrupted debate).
