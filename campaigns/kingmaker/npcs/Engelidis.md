# Engelidis

> **Role**: Naga; former Pitax palace resident
> **Location**: In transit — was being escorted to [[Nightmarch]] by [[Eri Malai]] post-war
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A naga. She was resident in the Pitax palace during [[King Irovetti]]'s rule, apparently tolerated or kept there for reasons not yet established.

## Personality

Completely unperturbed. When she casually revealed to [[Eri Malai]] that she had been having tea with his missing Master, that she had accidentally told Irovetti enough to force the Master into hiding, and that his Master had been waiting in the woods for someone to cast Guiding Star — she kept walking. Not a moment of hesitation, not a flicker of concern at the effect her words were having. She mentioned she "may have spilled a little tea" with the lightness of someone recounting a slightly embarrassing dinner party moment.

She remembers things in her own time: *"I think his name was Eri Malai? Maybe you can ask him if he has it."*

## Background

During her time in the Pitax palace, Engelidis had occasional tea conversations with [[Saint Flint|the Master of the Nagaji Monastic Order]]. She enjoyed these chats and, enjoying them, mentioned enough to [[King Irovetti]] about the Master's growing influence that Irovetti grew alarmed. The Master vanished soon after.

After Irovetti's fall, she was being escorted out of Pitax by [[Eri Malai]] toward [[Nightmarch]] when she mentioned all of this, in passing, while continuing to walk.

## Relationships

- [[Saint Flint]] — used to have tea with her in the palace; their conversations are what Engelidis accidentally reported to Irovetti
- [[Eri Malai]] — escorted her out of the city; was the unwitting recipient of information that turned his world upside down

## Session History

- Pitax palace: had tea conversations with the Master; inadvertently informed on her to Irovetti
- Post-war road to Nightmarch: revealed the Master's full situation to Eri Malai while walking, unperturbed
