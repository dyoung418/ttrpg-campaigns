# The Lantern King

> **Role**: The Eldest who broke [[Nyrissa]] and set the terms of her long penance — never on stage, always behind it
> **Status**: Alive; a background power, not yet a direct antagonist. Becomes the campaign's next threat if Nyrissa falls (Chapter 11).

## Summary

The Lantern King is one of the **Eldest** — the archfey lords of the First World — and the chief architect of [[Nyrissa]]'s fate. When Nyrissa reached to make herself an Eldest, he and his peers punished her: they sent the Jabberwock to ruin her, **tore out her capacity for love and forged it into the sword [[Briar|Briara]]**, and told her she could earn her soul back only by destroying **1,000 kingdoms** as *The Apology*. He is capricious, cruel, and fond of jokes at others' expense — the party's kingdom of [[Freehaven]] is meant to be the thousandth and final entry in his long, sadistic ledger.

## The Bargain *(and its contradictions)*

The exact terms of the Lantern King's bargain are a live mystery for the party — the accounts they've gathered **conflict**, and reconciling them is part of resolving Nyrissa's story:

- One reading: **Briara is the promised instrument of Nyrissa's death** — the blade foretold to herald the Heartless Queen's end.
- Another: **Nyrissa's love (and soul) would be restored** to her if she completed the Apology, i.e. the sword returned rather than turned against her.

In [[Session 103 - The Shape of Where I Used to Be|Session 103]], as [[Briar|Briara]] confessed she could feel [[Nyrissa]]'s nearness, the party dug back through their notes and prior interviews (including [[Evindra]]'s testimony) and found these stories **do not agree**. Whether the Lantern King ever meant Nyrissa to *succeed* — or simply strung her along for the cruelty of it — is unresolved.

## Significance

- He is the reason [[Briar|Briara]] exists and the reason [[Nyrissa]] does what she does.
- His curse frames the campaign's two endings: **slay Nyrissa** with Briara (a tragic death that is also a reunion) or **forgive/restore** her (returning the stolen soul).
- If Nyrissa is slain, **the Lantern King's frustration becomes the new threat** — Chapter 11 territory. See [[Chapter 10 - Sound of a Thousand Screams]] and [[Kingmaker Campaign Summary]].
