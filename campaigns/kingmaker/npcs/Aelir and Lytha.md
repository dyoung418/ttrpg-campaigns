# Aelir and Lytha

> **Role**: Riven's parents; the oath that defines him
> **Status**: Dead — killed in a demon ambush

## Background

Aelir (father) and Lytha (mother) were both skilled elven rangers in [[Kyonin]], and the center of [[Riven]]'s early life. They raised him on tales of ancestral bravery and the sacred duty to protect the elven lands — instilling a sense of purpose that shaped everything about him.

They were killed in an ambush by a demon when Riven was young. Their deaths shattered him, but also forged his oath: to carry on their tradition of service and protection.

**Aelir's longbow, [[Feywind]]**, is Riven's most prized possession — a direct physical connection to his father.

## Relationships

- [[Riven]] — their deaths defined his oath and set his entire life in motion
- [[Feywind]] — Aelir's longbow, now carried by Riven
