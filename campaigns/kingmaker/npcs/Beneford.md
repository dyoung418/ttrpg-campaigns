# Beneford

> **Role**: Court Bard; former student of [[Tol-Kin]]; teacher of [[Linzi]]
> **Location**: East — settled as Court Bard for a noble family
> **Status**: Alive

## Background

A student of [[Tol-Kin]] who has since settled into the role of Court Bard for a noble family somewhere in the East. [[Linzi]] trained under him before joining Freehaven as Emissary.

[[Tol-Kin]] scoffs at his settling down: *"A Bard who stays in one place is one whose stories grow stale."* [[Linzi]] says he seems happy there.

It was Linzi's request — on Beneford's behalf, or through his connection — that brought Tol-Kin to the Great Feast of Freehaven.

## GM Notes

- Beneford is largely a reference character. He connects Linzi to Tol-Kin and gives Tol-Kin a reason to have appeared at the feast.
- Not developed further unless the party travels east and encounters his employer's court.
