# Oleg Leveton

> **Role**: Proprietor of [[Oleg's Trading Post]]
> **Location**: [[Oleg's Trading Post]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A gruff, hard-working frontier trader who, with his wife [[Svetlana Leveton]], ran a small trading post on the northern edge of the Rostland Hinterlands and Greenbelt when the PCs first arrived in the Stolen Lands.

## Background

Oleg and Svetlana were among the very first people the PCs met when they set out to found their kingdom (Session 5). The trading post was being terrorized by a band of bandits in service to [[The Stag Lord]], who came regularly to extort goods. The PCs took up their cause, tracked the bandits to the [[Thorn River Camp]], and broke them — the opening move of the campaign that would become [[Freehaven]].

The trade town of [[Leveton]] later grew from the foundations of the trading post and carries the family name.

## Relationships

- [[Svetlana Leveton]] — his wife; ran the trading post alongside him
- [[Ekundayo]] — the PCs first met Ekundayo here at the post
