# The Old Beldame

> **Role**: Hermit witch; herbalist; alchemist
> **AKA**: Elga Verniex
> **Location**: [[Stolen Lands]] (isolated hut)
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

An eccentric, sharp-tongued old witch who keeps to herself deep in the Stolen Lands. She is skeptical of newcomers by default and does not suffer fools — her first impressions are stubborn and rarely revised.

## Personality

Blunt, sardonic, and deeply skeptical of anyone who can't sit still long enough to learn. She has a particular eye for people whose temperament doesn't match what they're claiming to want. Her teaching method is indirect — instructions framed as metaphors and sensory descriptions rather than measurements. Her warmth, such as it is, expresses itself in tolerance rather than affection.

## Background

The Old Beldame had a notable relationship with [[Astor]] during the years he stepped back from adventuring to build [[Freehaven]]. She taught him herbalism and rudimentary alchemy — skeptically. Her first reaction to his request was disbelief:

> *"You, who could not sit still long enough for a bee to land on you? You want to learn alchemy?"*

She describes results in poetic, sensory terms: a correct brew should look *"like the end of Spring."* Despite her doubts, she taught him, and Astor learned to tolerate boiling-hot liquids through the training.

She rejected Astor's invitation to his wedding. When he brought his first child to meet her, she threatened to eat the child. He brought his second child over anyway.

## Relationships

- [[Astor]] — her reluctant student in herbalism and alchemy; she doubted him from the start and has probably never revised her first impression of him as an oaf
