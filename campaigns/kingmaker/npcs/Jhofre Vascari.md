# Jhofre Vascari

> **Role**: Leader of the Vascari Bandit House
> **Location**: [[Pitax]]
> **Status**: Alive — ally of the PCs

## Description

Leader of the Vascari Bandit House, one of the criminal houses of [[Pitax]].

## Background

When the PCs infiltrated [[Pitax]] to overthrow [[King Irovetti]] (Session 80), Jhofre Vascari was among the contacts who aided them, lending the Vascari house's influence to the underground Fox Alliance alongside [[Gaspere Liacenza]]'s Liacenza house.

## Relationships

- [[Gaspere Liacenza]] — leader of the rival/allied Liacenza Bandit House
- [[King Irovetti]] — the tyrant the alliance worked to overthrow
