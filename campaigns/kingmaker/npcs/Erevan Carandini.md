# Erevan Carandini

> **Role**: Father of the [[Carandini Family]]
> **Faction**: [[Carandini Family]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A woodland elf; father to the nineteen Carandini children alongside his wife [[Elithya Carandini]].

## Personality

*Unestablished — offscreen backstory figure.* Together with [[Elithya Carandini]], stubborn enough to name all nineteen children in strict alphabetical sequence and see it through.

## Background

With [[Elithya Carandini]], parented the nineteen Carandini children and — half out of tradition, half out of sheer stubbornness — established the family's naming convention: each child named for the next consecutive letter of the alphabet plus "-ee" (Aee, Bee, [[Cee]], Dee, and so on). [[Lee]] is their twelfth child.

## Relationships

- [[Elithya Carandini]] — wife; co-parent of the nineteen children
- [[Lee]] — twelfth-born son, who left home and has sent no word since
- [[Aee]] — eldest child; now heads the family
- [[Cee]] — third-born; the family's Court Arcanist
- [[Carandini Family]] — patriarch of the line
