# Tamary Numesti

> **Role**: Daughter of [[Terrion Numesti]]; the Numesti hostage who did not escape
> **Location**: [[Fort Drevlev]] → taken by [[Armag Reborn]]
> **Status**: Alive — rescued by the PCs (Session 65)

## Description

Daughter of [[Terrion Numesti]] and sister of [[Kisandra Numesti]]. Where Kisandra escaped to warn [[Freehaven]], Tamary was the daughter taken as a hostage.

## Background

When the [[Tiger Lords]] demanded hostages from [[Hannis Drevlev]], Drevlev ordered [[Terrion Numesti]] to surrender a daughter. Terrion refused and was imprisoned; Tamary was held as a high-value hostage in [[Fort Drevlev]]. During the infiltration (Session 60 / [[RP 16 — Drevlev Infiltrations]]), [[Benedin]] forged the guard roster to place himself over her.

She and the other prisoners [[King Irovetti]] demanded to appease [[Armag Reborn]] were then taken by Armag. The PCs rescued the kidnapped daughter when they defeated Armag (Session 65).

## Relationships

- [[Terrion Numesti]] — her father
- [[Kisandra Numesti]] — her sister, who escaped to warn Freehaven
- [[Armag Reborn]] / [[Tiger Lords]] — took her captive
- [[Hannis Drevlev]] — whose treachery put her in danger
