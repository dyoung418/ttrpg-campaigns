# Hyram

> **Role**: Animal companion — very large goat
> **Location**: Wherever [[Benedin]] is
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A very large goat. Large enough that [[Benedin]] rides him into battle.

## Background

Benedin's animal companion. When Benedin fought alongside the party, he rode Hyram into combat. Hyram is impressively nimble for his size — he can leap from tiny stone outcroppings and ferry party members one at a time across narrow or treacherous passages. [[Riven]] "awkwardly mounts the goat" when offered a ride.

## Relationships

- [[Benedin]] — his person; Benedin rides him into battle
