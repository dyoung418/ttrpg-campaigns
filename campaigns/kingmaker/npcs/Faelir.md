## Background

A wise elven druid from [[Kyonin]] who took [[Riven]] under his wing during Riven's rise through the elven scouts. With Faelir's mentorship, Riven learned to channel his uncontrolled primal magic into spells and to temper his daring spirit with wisdom. Faelir dedicated himself to harmony — a philosophy that eventually clashed with Riven's success as a battlefield captain. Over time, their lives followed different paths and they drifted apart.

Faelir never appears to have been involved in the village incident or the court-martial, but he may know what happened to Riven.
