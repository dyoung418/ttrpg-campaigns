# Satinder Morne

> **Role**: Innkeeper; member of the Drevlev resistance
> **Location**: [[Fort Drevlev]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

The owner of an inn in [[Fort Drevlev]] and a member of the underground resistance against [[Hannis Drevlev]].

## Background

When the PCs moved to infiltrate Fort Drevlev (Session 60), they first took up residence in Satinder Morne's inn. As part of the resistance, Satinder gave them a foothold from which several of the PCs went on to enlist in the Drevlev guard and work their way toward Drevlev Keep.

## Relationships

- [[Hannis Drevlev]] — the tyrant the resistance opposed
- [[Fort Drevlev]] — location of the inn and the resistance
