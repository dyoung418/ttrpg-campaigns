# The Horned Hunter

**Creature 18** | Unique, CN, Medium, Fey (Variant satyr ranger)

[[Nyrissa]]'s enforcer sent on Day 9 of [[The Month of Destruction]] with one mission: steal [[Briar]] and return it to Nyrissa via the Castle of Knives portal.

> [!success] Deceased — killed by the PCs (Session 100)
> The Horned Hunter and his Jotund Trolls were the bloom fight in progress when [[Linzi]]'s Sending arrived with news of [[Lyriel]]'s abduction ([[Session 100 - She Dropped Your Letter]]). He died in that battle without ever claiming [[Briar]] — and with him ended Nyrissa's **Fey Conduit** surveillance through his senses.

## Stats

- **AC** 42 | **HP** 350
- **Fort** +32, **Ref** +30, **Will** +30 (juggernaut)
- **Weaknesses**: cold iron 15
- **Perception** +35; low-light vision
- **Languages**: Common, Jotun, Sylvan

**Skills**: Acrobatics +35, Athletics +35, First World Lore +32, Nature +33, Stealth +35, Survival +33

**Items**: +2 greater striking grievous thundering darkwood composite shortbow (20 arrows), hunting horn, wineskin

## Key Abilities

- **Fey Conduit**: Nyrissa watches through his senses. She observes the PCs' tactics and resources the entire time he operates on the Material Plane. This link ends if he claims Briar.
- **Hunt Prey**: +2 circumstance on Perception (Seek) and Survival (Track) vs. prey; ignore second-range-increment penalty.
- **Masterful Hunter**: −3/−6 MAP vs. hunted prey (−2/−4 with agile); +2d8 precision on first hit per round.
- **Bloom Step**: Ignores all non-magical difficult terrain and bloom terrain.
- **Play the Pipes** (3 actions, auditory, primal): Satyr pipe effect via hunting horn. DC 37.
- **Fleet Performer** (reaction): Steps or Strides when he Plays the Pipes.
- **Primal Innate Spells** DC 37: 8th *suggestion*, *tree stride*; 4th *charm*, *fear*, *sleep*.

## Tactics

Appears at the Castle of Knives with 3× Jotund Trolls (CR 15). Immediately seeks the PCs, leaving devastation behind to draw them out.

1. Opens with **Play the Pipes** to position for maximum effect.
2. Attacks with shortbow, focusing on **whoever carries Briar**.
3. If the Briar wielder is downed, he immediately claims the sword and uses **tree stride** to flee to the Castle of Knives and return it to Nyrissa.
4. If PCs don't seem to care about their people, the Hunt may infiltrate homes and try to ambush isolated PCs.

## What Happens If He Gets Briar

If Nyrissa gains Briar, she will not send the lesser jabberwock on Day 28 (she no longer fears the prophecy). The PCs will find Briar in her personal chambers (Fable M7d) when they reach the House at the Edge of Time.
