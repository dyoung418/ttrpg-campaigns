## Background

A demon who invaded Kyonin. The elves repelled him from the heart of their homeland but failed to drive him out entirely. He holds the Tanglebriar — a blighted, corrupted forest south of Kyonin — and commands undead forces that continuously threaten the elven lands.

[[Riven]] fought his undead for over fifty years before leaving Kyonin. Even now, Treerazer still holds the Tanglebriar. The elves have not reclaimed it.

Riven's framing: *"Kyonin is beautiful and full of exquisite art, but we let our guard down once, and Treerazer nearly claimed the land we hold sacred."* He uses this as a warning about what happens when corruption is left to fester — the direct motivation for his resolve at Vordakai's lair.

## Relationships

- [[Riven]] — Riven fought his undead forces for 50+ years; Treerazer is the reason Riven's vigilance against undead and demonic corruption is so visceral
- [[Avennara]] — the elven city that has resisted his expansion
