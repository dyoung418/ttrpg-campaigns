# Atlee Quinge

> **Role**: Director of the Red Crescent Troop (theater company)
> **Location**: [[Pitax]]
> **Status**: Alive — ally of the PCs

## Description

Director of a theater troupe in [[Pitax]] called the Red Crescent Troop.

## Background

When the PCs infiltrated [[Pitax]] to overthrow [[King Irovetti]] (Session 80), Atlee Quinge was one of the city contacts who proved helpful, lending the cover and reach of the theater troupe to the underground Fox Alliance.

## Relationships

- [[Gaspere Liacenza]] — fellow Pitaxian contact in the Fox Alliance
- [[King Irovetti]] — the tyrant the alliance worked to overthrow
