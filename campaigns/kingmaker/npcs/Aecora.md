## Personality

Fiercely loyal to her people and protective of them — [[Riven]]'s words. He admires her for this. She escorts outsiders through Nomen territory through her warriors for her people's safety, not out of hospitality.

## Background

Leader of the Nomen Centaurs in the [[Nomen Heights]]. Her people were desecrated when centaur skins were taken by parties connected to the [[Varnhold]] situation — Riven found this sickening and regretted not properly honoring the dead with burial, intending to do so on the return journey.

After the Vordakai affair (in which her people's relationship with the party was shaped), the Nomen Centaurs allied with [[Freehaven]] and [[Ashenfel]] was integrated into the kingdom.

## Relationships

- [[Nomen Centaurs]] — her people; she leads them
- [[Xamanthe]] — her daughter; was captured and held by [[Vordakai]]; rescued by the party
- [[Riven]] — he deeply admires her leadership and her loyalty to her people; sees her as a model worth respecting
- [[Vordakai]] — the lich whose actions were intertwined with the desecration of centaur remains at Varnhold; captured and cursed Xamanthe
