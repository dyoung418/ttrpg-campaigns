# Imeckus Stroon

> **Role**: Powerful magician; brother-in-law to [[Hannis Drevlev]]
> **Location**: [[Fort Drevlev]]
> **Status**: Deceased — defeated by the PCs

## Description

A powerful magician who stood at [[Hannis Drevlev]]'s side, and the brother of the Baroness [[Pavetta Stroon-Drevlev]].

## Background

When the PCs assaulted Drevlev Keep to bring down [[Hannis Drevlev]] (Session 60), Imeckus fought alongside him as his magical muscle. The PCs defeated him in the same engagement that ended Drevlev's reign.

## Relationships

- [[Hannis Drevlev]] — his brother-in-law and ally
- [[Pavetta Stroon-Drevlev]] — his sister, Drevlev's wife
- [[Fort Drevlev]] — seat of the family's power
