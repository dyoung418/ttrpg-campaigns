## Background

One of [[Lucas]]'s foxfolk ancestors, more distant than [[Great Aunt Sumi]] or [[Great Uncle Olaf]]. Was the family matriarch for approximately 60 years after her own mother died — long enough that her power and reputation defined the Sato clan for a generation. She developed fierce defensiveness of the kitsune clan over that time, calling down wrath upon all who sought to persecute them. She had a specific, deep hatred for undead and creatures of shadow — a hatred that has grown more active as the party faces [[Vordakai]].

It was Gina who first appeared to [[Lucas]] after [[Great Aunt Sumi]]'s death — about two weeks after Sumi began whispering to him from her old books. Gina told Lucas he was special and that the ancestors had chosen him. He thought he was going crazy and ignored it for months.

Grants him both the **Ancestral Touch** and **Inner Radiance Torrent** Oracle Ancestor abilities when called upon — two abilities from one ancestor, reflecting her exceptional power in life.
