# Eri Malai

> **Also known as**: Saint Flint *(alias — taken from his Master without her knowledge)*
> **Role**: Nagaji monk; junior member of the monastic order; companion to the party
> **Faction**: [[Nagaji Monastic Order]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A nagaji monk who arrived with the Pitax delegation at the Grand Tournament of Freehaven, presenting himself as "Saint Flint." He was secretly opposed to [[King Irovetti]] and joined the PCs in resisting him, traveling with the group through several adventures.

He carried [[The Starlight Staff]] as a symbol of office — not understanding until much later that it was a magical instrument specifically left behind to call his Master back.

## Personality

Well-intentioned but not always well-judged. His Master called him "one of her favorite idiot students" — the warmth and the exasperation both clearly genuine. He improvised an entire identity, a public role, and apparently a whole period of leading his order, all because his Master left no note. He froze in place when [[Engelidis]] mentioned meeting another nagaji, then had to run to catch up to a naga who had kept walking.

He gives sermons. He escorts important figures out of cities. He calls stars by which direction they'll guide someone home. He's earnest, capable, and occasionally clueless in ways that seem to follow him.

## Background

Eri Malai is a student of the [[Nagaji Monastic Order]] and a follower of [[Saint Flint|his Master]]. When the Master vanished — forced to flee [[Pitax]] because [[King Irovetti]] had grown fearful of her influence — she left behind her staff, her vestments, and no note. Eri Malai, facing a leaderless and frightened order, made a decision: he would take her name and her role until she returned, so that others would not think them headless.

He arrived at the Grand Tournament as part of the Pitax delegation, presenting himself as "Saint Flint." There he revealed his opposition to Irovetti and joined the PCs, fighting with them through the Pitax War and beyond.

After Irovetti's fall, Eri Malai helped the order come out of hiding, searched the city for his missing Master, found nothing — and then learned everything from [[Engelidis]] on the road to [[Nightmarch]]. He used the [[The Starlight Staff|Starlight Staff]] that night, pointing it at a star and casting Guiding Star, intending to call his Master home.

**The reunion was one week later.** His Master walked into the middle of a church service, took back her staff, and bopped him on the head with it.

## Relationships

- [[Saint Flint]] — his Master; the woman whose name, robes, and staff he borrowed; she's stern with him and clearly fond of him
- [[Engelidis]] — the naga who revealed where his Master had gone, casually and devastatingly, while continuing to walk
- [[King Irovetti]] — the tyrant he came to undermine at the tournament; now defeated
- [[The Starlight Staff]] — the object he carried as a symbol of status without understanding its true purpose
