# Brumble

> **Role**: [[Riven]]'s bear companion (retired from active adventure)
> **Location**: [[Nightmarch]]
> **Status**: Alive — fat and happy

## Description

A grizzly bear, grown large and round. "He's grown too fat," Riven says, laughing. Happy in Nightmarch.

## Background

Riven found Brumble as an orphaned cub caught in a trap, somewhere during his wanderings after fleeing [[The Few]]. He saved the cub and named him Brumble. Brumble ended up saving Riven "at his darkest time" — the lowest point of Riven's self-exile before he found purpose again.

When the party's path grew too dangerous, Riven left Brumble in [[Nightmarch]]. He misses him, but is genuinely glad the bear is safe, comfortable, and happy.

## Relationships

- [[Riven]] — his person; Riven loves him more than he says out loud; left him in Nightmarch to protect him

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> - A tool for Riven's emotional life: mentions of Brumble reveal what Riven values and what he's sacrificed for this quest.
> - Brumble is in Nightmarch and could be encountered if the party returns.

## Session History

- Great Feast of Freehaven: Present and stretched out by the fire in the grand hall. [[Ekundayo]] nudged him to produce a deep roar that silenced the hall before Riven's speech — sparking laughter first. Fat, warm, and happy.
