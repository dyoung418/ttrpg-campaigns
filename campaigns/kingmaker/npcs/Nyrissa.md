# Nyrissa

**Creature 23** | Unique, CE, Medium, Fey, Nymph
*The Heartless Queen / Queen of Forgotten Time / Count's Last Mistress*

The ultimate antagonist of Kingmaker. A tragic villain — once a brilliant, passionate Nymph Queen who loved deeply, now hollowed out and single-minded after the Eldest tore her emotions away. She has been systematically destroying civilizations for centuries with the mechanical focus of someone trying to end their own damnation.

## Stats (Extreme 19 Encounter)

- **AC**: 47 | **HP**: high (full stats on page 602)
- **Perception** +41
- **Initiative**: Perception +41

## Tactics

Nyrissa watches the PCs' entire progress through her House from Fable M7c. She enters combat prepared:
1. **Rounds 1–3**: Uses Quickened Casting to layer defensive spells: *freedom of movement*, *mirror image*, *true seeing*.
2. **In combat**: Stays at range, uses spells and Focus Beauty. Targets the Briar wielder aggressively.
3. **If reduced to <200 HP or significantly threatened**: Uses *wish* to duplicate *banishment* (9th level) to force PCs back to the Material Plane.
4. **If reduced to <100 HP**: Switches Fables to recover. Returns 1 minute later if PCs remain.
5. **If cornered / can't escape**: Calls for a truce. This opens the Chapter 11 dialogue.

If Thousandbreaths has been **uprooted** before this fight, she starts the combat **frightened 1** (drops if PCs retreat). Forgive Foe DC reduces to **46**.

**Fable M7c advantage**: She can observe every room and kill any ally she sees who has been controlled or is cooperating with the PCs (single Envision action). She will alert surviving minions to intercept the PCs at critical moments.

## The Shame Mechanic

Nyrissa bears the Shame condition. What triggers it and what Forgive Foe does to her is detailed in Chapter 11, but the DC is:
- Standard: **50**
- After Thousandbreaths uprooted: **46**

## The Two Endings

**Slay Nyrissa** — Thousandbreaths (if not already uprooted) erupts and transforms into the Castle of Knives ruins. Her body rises on a pyre of black lightning and vanishes in black clouds. All surviving Thousandbreaths denizens scatter. The Lantern King's frustrations become the new threat (Chapter 11).

**Forgive Nyrissa** — Possible via Forgive Foe (critical success) or if she calls for a truce. Her soul is restored when [[Briar]] is returned to her or used in a specific way (Chapter 11). She can become an ally against the Lantern King.

## Background

See [[Kingmaker Campaign Summary]] for full backstory. Short version: she earned a punishment from the Eldest (chiefly the Lantern King) for trying to make herself an Eldest. They sent the Jabberwock to destroy her, then tore out her emotions and forged them into [[Briar]], telling her she could earn her soul back by capturing 1,000 kingdoms as *The Apology*. She has 999 so far. The PCs' kingdom is meant to be the last.

She has been watching the PCs since they arrived in the Stolen Lands, subtly helping threats against them to make their kingdom stronger and more worthy of being the 1,000th kingdom. She is genuinely surprised and increasingly terrified that they might actually stop her.
