# Harrim

> **Role**: Viceroy of [[Freehaven]]
> **Location**: [[Nightmarch]]
> **Faction**: [[Freehaven]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A middle-aged dwarf cleric with a bald head and a long, salt-and-pepper beard.

## Personality

Calm, level-headed, and surprisingly good at talking to people — until he inevitably starts rambling about the end of days. As a servant of [[Groetus]] (god of the end times), Harrim is prone to declarations like *"Abandon hope and embrace the inevitable."* He means it in a comforting way, more or less.

## Background

Harrim answered [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]]'s call for heroes after learning of possible lost dwarven ruins in the Stolen Lands. He hoped to rediscover them before they crumbled away entirely — or before the world ends, whichever comes first. He joined the party's expedition and has since taken up the role of Viceroy in the [[Freehaven]] government, operating out of [[Nightmarch]].

His interest in the dwarven ruins may connect to the history of [[Bronzeshield Hold]].

## Relationships

- [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]] — met her at the original feast; answered her call
- [[Freehaven]] — serves as Viceroy in the kingdom government
- [[Bronzeshield Hold]] — potentially tied to his search for dwarven ruins

## GM Notes

- Serves as Viceroy — a government role not held by any of the PCs; position should be reflected in Freehaven's leadership
- His doom-and-gloom preaching is balanced by genuine warmth and pastoral skill
