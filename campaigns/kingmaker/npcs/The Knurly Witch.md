# The Knurly Witch

**Creature 20** | Unique, CE, Large, Hag, Humanoid (Variant female annis hag, cleric of Gyronna)

One of [[Nyrissa]]'s few genuine friends — or as close as Nyrissa gets. An annis hag cleric of Gyronna (goddess of spite and cruelty) who stumbled into Thousandbreaths 220 years ago and chose servitude over becoming linnorm food. She has abandoned her birth name entirely. Thousandbreaths has accepted her as a native.

**Locations**: First encountered in **Glade H (the Knurly House)**. If she flees via *word of recall*, she retreats to **Cellar K16** in the [[House at the Edge of Time]].

## Stats

- **AC** 45 | **HP** 324
- **Fort** +32, **Ref** +35, **Will** +38; +1 status to all saves vs. magic; **Resolve** (successful Will save → critical success)
- **Resistances**: physical 20 (except bludgeoning)
- **Perception** +38; darkvision
- **Languages**: Aklo, Common, Jotun, Sylvan
- **Skills**: Acrobatics +35, Athletics +34, Deception +37, Intimidate +34, Nature +36, Occultism +35, Religion +38

**Items**: Religious symbol of Gyronna, wand of slaying (7th)

**Key Spells** (DC 44): 10th *harm* ×7, *summon plant or fungus*; 9th *heal*, *massacre*, *telepathic demand*; 8th *dispel magic*, *flame strike*; 7th *divine decree*, *regenerate*, *divine wrath*; 6th *blade barrier*, *feeblemind*, *paranoia*; 4th *air walk*, *discern lies*; rituals: *blight*, *word of recall*

**Word of Recall**: Pre-bound to K16 in the House at the Edge of Time.

## Tactics

**Glade H encounter**: She doesn't immediately attack. Invites the PCs to share "tea" (crow's blood + powdered maggots; DC 40 Perception to notice; DC 38 Fort or sickened 2–4 on a sip). She plays a dishonest information exchange: for every question she asks about the PCs (powers, goals, weaknesses), she answers one about Nyrissa — but **every answer she gives is a lie**.

Combat order:
1. Casts *air walk* and flies upward out of melee reach.
2. Casts *summon plant or fungus* to summon a wemmuth.
3. Ranged spells against the PCs.
4. At ≤250 HP: casts *regenerate*.
5. At ≤100 HP: activates *word of recall* and flees to K16.

**K16 encounter**: If cornered here after retreating, she fights to the death.

**K15 servant wing**: If the Knurly Witch is in K16 and the Ghostly Guards in K15 raise an alarm, she joins the fight and uses *harm* to keep the ghosts healed.
