## Personality

Reserved or difficult to read — at least for [[Pavel]], who noted that humanoids remain as much a mystery to him as ever when trying to figure out what would interest or impress Ekundayo. Pavel ended up connecting with Ekundayo's dog instead.

Has a dog companion named Dog.

## Background

Ekundayo was instrumental in the battle that overthrew [[The Stag Lord]] early in the campaign. After the victory, instead of moving on, he chose to settle in [[Nightmarch]].

There he met and proposed to [[Kisandra Numesti]], daughter of [[Terrion Numesti]], a general serving [[Hannis Drevlev]]. When Drevlev conspired with [[King Irovetti]] and the [[Tiger Lords]] against [[Freehaven]], the Tiger Lords forced hostages from Drevlev; Terrion refused to surrender a daughter and was imprisoned, and Kisandra's **sister** was taken captive. Kisandra herself escaped and rode to [[Bronzeshield Hold]] to warn the party. The PCs broke the attacking army, freed Terrion, and later rescued her captured sister from the Tiger Lords.

## Relationships

- Dog — his dog companion; the bridge between Ekundayo and [[Pavel]]
- [[Kisandra Numesti]] — the woman he proposed to in Nightmarch; daughter of Drevlev's general [[Terrion Numesti]]; she escaped to warn Freehaven of the attack
- [[Hannis Drevlev]] — his treachery (and the demand on Kisandra's father) put her family in danger
- [[The Stag Lord]] — Ekundayo fought against him; his defeat was partly due to Ekundayo's involvement
- [[Pavel]] — Pavel tried and failed to figure out how to impress Ekundayo; ended up connecting through Dog instead; in that moment Pavel opened up about his kennel childhood
