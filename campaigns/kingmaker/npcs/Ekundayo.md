## Personality

Reserved or difficult to read — at least for [[Pavel]], who noted that humanoids remain as much a mystery to him as ever when trying to figure out what would interest or impress Ekundayo. Pavel ended up connecting with Ekundayo's dog instead.

Has a dog companion named Dog.

## Background

Ekundayo was instrumental in the battle that overthrew [[The Stag Lord]] early in the campaign. After the victory, instead of moving on, he chose to settle in [[Nightmarch]].

There he met a woman he fell in love with and proposed to. She was a relative of [[Hannis Drevlev]]. When Drevlev conspired with [[King Irovetti]] and the Tiger Lords against [[Freehaven]], the Tiger Lords took the woman captive as leverage in the power struggle between Drevlev and themselves. The PCs rescued her as part of the campaign against the Tiger Lords and Drevlev.

- [[Hannis Drevlev]] — relative of the woman Ekundayo proposed to; Drevlev's treachery put her in danger
