# Halvik

> **Role**: Sergeant under [[Cassandra]]; bellow that carries through a gale
> **Status**: Alive (last seen at Willowshade where Cassandra died)
> **Faction**: [[Silverstep]] Guard

## Description

Broad and blunt. Called cadence in both the meadow fight and the Willowshade wheel. Was beside [[Cassandra]] when she fell — she drove her halberd home so Halvik could thrust through the shield rim.

## Relationships

- [[Cassandra]] — his commander; he was at her side when she died at [[Willowshade]]
- [[Senn]] — fellow sergeant under Cassandra
- [[Riven]] — strategic commander at Willowshade

> "Hadrik" (used once in the council scene of [[RP 22 — The Rest of the Battle of Silverstep]]) is confirmed by the GM as the same man — **Halvik** is the canonical spelling; Hadrik is kept as an alias for search.
