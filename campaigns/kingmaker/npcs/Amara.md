## Background

A wandering priest of Sarenrae (goddess of redemption and healing) whom [[Riven]] encountered in [[Mivon]] after fleeing [[The Few]]. In a period of deep despair, Amara spoke to Riven of second chances and the power to atone — rekindling a spark of hope he thought extinguished.

Amara's words touched Riven but didn't fully penetrate. He acknowledged her wisdom yet still clung to the belief that redemption could only come from great external deeds — not from within. This is the central tension in his arc.

## Relationships

- [[Riven]] — gave him hope when he had none; a guiding voice he hasn't fully heeded

## GM Notes

- Amara represents the voice of Sarenrae's doctrine: redemption is real, available to all, and comes through genuine change — not just deeds.
- She could appear again as a mirror to show Riven how far (or how little) he has grown.
- Does she know what became of him? Is she still wandering Mivon, or has she heard of the kingdom-builder of the Stolen Lands?
