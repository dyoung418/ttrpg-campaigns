# Armag Reborn

> **Role**: Legendary barbarian resurrected; defeated at [[Fort Drevlev]]
> **Status**: Defeated

## Background

A legendary barbarian figure who was reborn and wielded [[Ovinrbaane]], a legendary weapon. The party defeated Armag Reborn during the [[Fort Drevlev]] conflict. [[Benedin]] was seriously wounded by Ovinrbaane in the fight.

Referenced by [[Riven]] as one of the party's major accomplishments: *"you freed Drevlev and stopped Armag Reborn."*
