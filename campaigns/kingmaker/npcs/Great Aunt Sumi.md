# Great Aunt Sumi

> **Role**: Lucas's ancestor; powerful healer and mage (deceased)
> **Location**: Spirit realm — but meddlesome enough to appear when she has opinions
> **Status**: Deceased (ancestor)

## Description

An elderly foxfolk woman with white skin, long white hair that appears to blow in an unseen wind, sharp pointed ears, deeply wrinkled features, and distinctive orange eyes with fox-shaped pupils rather than round ones. Appears entirely white except for those unsettling orange eyes. Has an air of sharp disapproval.

[[Pavel]] described her exactly: *"all wrinkly and sharp ... long white hair that looked like it was blowing in the wind ... crazy pointed ears ... orange eyes ... pupils weren't round neither. I recognize fox eyes when I see 'em."*

## Personality

No bedside manner. Makes people feel guilty for needing healing in the first place. Glares at patients rather than comforting them. Her famous observation after saving [[Lucas]]'s mother: *"I hope you keep that little kit around, Cana, because I had to interrupt my canasta game to come save her and you..."*

Meddlesome. Has strong opinions about how [[Lucas]] should use his abilities and dislikes when he relies on non-magical methods. Will "stick her whiskers" into his decisions given any excuse. Disdains non-magic approaches to healing.

Despite this, she is genuinely powerful and genuinely cares — in a very prickly way.

**More than medicine:** She is also a renowned cook, and claims expertise in matters of the heart — specifically offering dating advice to kitsune bachelors, noting she *"fought off many suitors in her day."* Lucas has only ever treated her as a medical reference, which she resents.

**What she wants from Lucas:** To be communed with on occasion just to chat, not only when he needs something. Her birthday matters to her. She is aware he forgot it five years running (the last time he communed on her birthday, he only wanted a diarrhea remedy). Significant dates: her birthday, Nine Tails, New Moon. She would like to be addressed properly — "O Great Aunt Sumi" — with emphasis on "Great." Three words, not one slurred together.

**Medical dispute with Lucas:** She advocates for black thornleaf bark on infected wounds. Lucas insists powdered otan root is superior. [[Pavel]] sides with Sumi: thornleaf *is* effective if you steep it in hot water for at least 2 hours before drying and powdering. Lucas's objection is largely about convenience and tradition.

## Background

A real historical figure, not a legendary ancestor. Lucas's father knew her in life. She saved Lucas's mother Cana's life during the birth of Lucas's sister. She never let Cana forget it.

[[Lucas]] knew her personally as a child. She was terrifying to all the children in the family — all of them. But she never turned anyone away who needed help. She taught Lucas to use a healer's kit.

She died of wasting fever when Lucas was 12. He was the only one present and tried to help, but the best he could do was make her comfortable. Her death hit him hard: he threw himself into studying healing, read all her old books on herbs and wound care, even considered becoming a cleric. About two weeks into this, he realized her voice was coming through the books — telling him things not written in them, things she'd never told anyone. This was the beginning of his oracle awakening.

She appeared to [[Pavel]] during the battle with the Stag Lord, manifesting briefly through [[Lucas]] as he healed Pavel — visible to Pavel as he hovered near death, before Lucas's face resolved over hers as the healing took hold. [[Lucas]] was "beginning to lose himself to the ancestors" during the battle; Sumi saw an opportunity to meddle and took it.

## Relationships

- [[Lucas]] — great-nephew; she has strong opinions about how he should operate and appears when she disapproves; he has frozen his view of her as the terrifying 11-year-old version and hasn't let it evolve
- [[Great Uncle Olaf]] — romantically involved (or intending to be); she calls him to act on her behalf; he calls her "Sum Sum"; she was in a wedding dress in the ancestor vision; she hits him over the head when he fails her
- Cana (Lucas's mother) — saved her life; never stopped mentioning it
- Lucas's father — knew Sumi in life; used to tell stories about getting glared at by her
- [[Pavel]] — she sent [[Great Uncle Olaf]] to use Pavel as a mouthpiece and advocate for her cause; she trusts Pavel enough to work through him; this represents a significant shift from her earlier glare of disapproval

## Plot Hooks Involved

- [[Pavel's Parentage]] — Sumi's appearance is what prompted Pavel to ask Lucas about his ancestors, which led to Pavel revealing he never knew his father and wants to meet his father's family

## Ancestor Ability

Grants [[Lucas]] the **Healing** Oracle Ancestor ability.

## GM Notes

> [!secret] GM Only
> - Sumi is a useful tool to make [[Lucas]] uncomfortable — she appears when he does something she disapproves of.
> - Her appearance to [[Pavel]] is a significant moment: it cracked open Pavel's guarded exterior and led to his most vulnerable disclosure yet.
> - She "disdains non-magic ways of doing things" — this is a good handle if Lucas ever relies on mundane methods.
> - She is close enough a relative that the Stolen Lands' unusual attunement to the spirit world makes her easier to contact than older legendary ancestors.
