# Amiri

> **Role**: Adventurer; settled resident of [[Nightmarch]]
> **Location**: [[Nightmarch]]
> **Faction**: [[Freehaven]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A female human barbarian. Big personality, bigger arms.

## Personality

Competitive, hard-drinking, and direct. At Jamandi's feast she arm-wrestled [[Astor]] and put down more beers than anyone at the table. Exactly the kind of person who fits in with a group of adventurers trying to carve a kingdom out of the wilderness.

## Background

One of the adventurers who answered [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]]'s call to settle the Stolen Lands — she was present at the original feast in Restov. She befriended the party during the evening and chose to follow them rather than strike out on her own. Now settled in [[Nightmarch]] as part of the kingdom the PCs built.

## Relationships

- [[Astor]] — arm-wrestled him at the feast; a memorable introduction
- [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]] — met her at the original feast in Restov
- [[Freehaven]] — her kingdom; she chose to settle here
