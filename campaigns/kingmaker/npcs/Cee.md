# Cee

> **Role**: Court Arcanist of the [[Carandini Family]]
> **Faction**: [[Carandini Family]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

Third-born of the nineteen Carandini children and the family's Court Arcanist. A woodland elf and a practicing spellcaster.

## Personality

As a child, delighted in needling her younger brother [[Lee]] — flashing illusions and minor hexes dressed up as "lessons," cantrips cast just to watch him flinch, tricks explained only after he'd already been humiliated by them. Whether this was casual cruelty or affection with a sharp edge, it left a mark.

## Background

Grew up as the third of nineteen siblings and rose to become the family's Court Arcanist. Her "teaching" of [[Lee]] — years of magic played on him at his own expense — is the direct source of his private, unshakeable conviction that magic isn't real: that it's a con, and every spellcaster is in on the joke. Lee has never let that go, and outshining Cee specifically is one of his core motivations.

## Relationships

- [[Lee]] — younger brother (twelfth-born); the sibling she needled with illusions; he left home partly to prove swordplay and nerve beat her "magic," and privately still wants to outshine her
- [[Aee]] — eldest sibling; head of the family
- [[Erevan Carandini]] — father
- [[Elithya Carandini]] — mother
