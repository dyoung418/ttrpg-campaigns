# Great Uncle Olaf

> **Role**: Lucas's ancestor; poltergeist-type spirit (deceased)
> **Location**: Spirit realm
> **Status**: Deceased (ancestor)

## Description

A gray-haired kitsune in farmer's coveralls. Unprepossessing — the kind of ancestor you'd find tending a garden, not calling down wrath upon persecutors. His role in the family appears to be as a willing errand-runner for [[Great Aunt Sumi]].

## Personality

Cowardly under pressure (especially when facing [[Cat]]). Enthusiastic and committed when not terrified. He plays along with schemes willingly — almost too willingly — and is easily dominated by Sumi when things go wrong. She beats him on the head; he petrifies; she keeps beating. He called her "Sum Sum" with genuine warmth before the roar.

## Background

One of [[Lucas]]'s foxfolk ancestors. Romantically involved with [[Great Aunt Sumi]] — or at least pursuing that status. He attempted to negotiate on her behalf by possessing [[Pavel]] during Lucas's night camp dream/vision, speaking her grievances and demands in Pavel's voice. The plan was his or hers or both.

When [[Cat]] detected the possession and grew to twice her normal size, Olaf's telepathic message to Lucas came back screaming: *"No I'm not okay you fool boy! That BEAST isn't supposed to be here! I can feel it reaching for me. It's… that's impossible!"* His terror of Cat is absolute and suggests Cat is something the ancestors genuinely cannot handle.

When the vision broke, Sumi was beating him on the head shouting *"GET US OUT OF HERE, YOU FOOL!"*

## Relationships

- [[Lucas]] — great-nephew; source of his Poltergeist ability; Olaf tried to advocate for Sumi's better treatment by Lucas
- [[Great Aunt Sumi]] — romantic interest ("Sum Sum"); he acts as her agent and advocate; she dominates the relationship
- [[Great Great Aunt Gina]] — fellow ancestor in the Sato family spirit realm
- [[Cat]] — deeply, existentially terrified of her; she can perceive him and reach for him; he calls her "that BEAST" and says her presence in the mortal world is *"impossible"*
