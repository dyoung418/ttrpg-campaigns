# Svetlana Leveton

> **Role**: Co-proprietor of [[Oleg's Trading Post]]
> **Location**: [[Oleg's Trading Post]]
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

Wife of [[Oleg Leveton]] and co-runner of the frontier trading post the PCs found on first entering the Stolen Lands.

## Background

Together with Oleg, Svetlana was among the first people the PCs met in the Stolen Lands (Session 5). The couple were being preyed upon by bandits loyal to [[The Stag Lord]]; the PCs' decision to help them was the first thread that grew into the kingdom of [[Freehaven]]. The town of [[Leveton]] later took the family name.

## Relationships

- [[Oleg Leveton]] — her husband; ran the trading post together
- [[The Stag Lord]] — his bandits extorted the post
