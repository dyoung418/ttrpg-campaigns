# Bird

> **Role**: Animal companion — bird of prey
> **Location**: Wherever [[Pavel]] is
> **Status**: Alive

## Description

A bird of prey. Named "Bird." Pavel names his animals with the same economy he brings to everything else.

## Relationships

- [[Pavel]] — her person

## GM Notes

- Like [[Cat]], Bird reflects Pavel's preference for animals over people: straightforward, honest, no pretense.
- A bird of prey offers Pavel aerial scouting — fitting for the kingdom's Warden.
- Pavel's Beastmaster dedication supports multiple animal companions.
- **Health concern**: Pavel noted post-Vordakai that Bird isn't thriving — undersized for his age and species. Pavel plans to work on this when he returns to [[Nightmarch]]. [[Riven]] suggested fresh air and a proper feast might help (comparing to how Brumble ate his way through growth spurts). Worth returning to as a subplot or character moment.
