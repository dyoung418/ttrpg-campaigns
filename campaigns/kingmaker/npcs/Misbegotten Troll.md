# Misbegotten Troll

**Creature 18** | Guardian of [[Glade I — The Baleful Lantern]]

A regenerating fey-corrupted troll that holds the Baleful Lantern in [[Thousandbreaths]], commanding a pack of eight Black Smilodons. When bloodied, it retreats into the beehive tower to heal — then returns whole.

## Tactics

- **Returns fully healed** after withdrawing into the Lantern to regenerate.
- **Burrowing emergence** (GM table ruling, Session 101): rather than crossing open ground into the party's ranged fire, it tunnels through the glade's corrupted earth (**Burrow Speed 30**) and erupts beneath the back line. Its emergence Strike grants a free Trip or Grab on a hit; adjacent creatures make a DC 36 Reflex save or fall prone. Telegraphed a round early (trembling ground, sifting dust).
- Commands the **8× Black Smilodons** (CR 14) — corrupted First World echoes of [[Cat]].
