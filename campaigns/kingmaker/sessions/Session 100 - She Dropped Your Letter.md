## Step 5 — Fantastic Locations

### The Byway — Iron Arch of the Heartless Queen
*(Entry point; Area A of [[Thousandbreaths]])*
- Two women-statues raising swords shaped like [[Briar]] — when [[Pavel]] walks through carrying Briar, the sword and both statues blaze green; [[Nyrissa]] notices
- Eternal twilight; the sun has not moved in centuries
- The woods press close on both sides — off the path, thorns draw blood with every step

### Glade I — The Baleful Lantern
- A beehive-shaped tower lit by decapitated heads used as lanterns, casting sick orange light across the glade
- The anchor is the building itself (AC 30, Hardness 20, HP 400, BT 200) — the glade ends only when the structure falls
- Eight Black Smilodons prowl the perimeter — fey big cats, corrupted First World predators; [[Cat]] is here too

### Glade J — The High Folly
- Mossy stone statues of a petrified First World army surrounding a 300-foot white stone tower
- [[Ilthuliak]] atop the tower — she sees them coming; she opens with *time stop* and the party has one round of uncontested action before she moves
- The acid lake below; a breath weapon (22d6 acid, 100-ft line) from a creature that circles overhead

### The Bridge to the House at the Edge of Time
- A long stone bridge over a lake so dark it has no bottom; the far shore flickers between mountain ranges and open starfield
- [[Tarlaxian]] (elite tarn linnorm, CR 21) coiled at the far end — blocks the bridge, won't pursue into the House
- The [[House at the Edge of Time]] visible across the water: carved laughing, frowning, and frightened faces on every door
