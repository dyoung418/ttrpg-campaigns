# RP 22 — The Rest of the Battle of Silverstep

> **Source**: RP22 (battle vignette in four movements; written by Wes/Riven)
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Riven]] (with the Rangers; [[Cassandra]] and the Silverstep Guard)
> **Setting**: The eve and day of the decisive Battle of [[Willowshade]], outside [[Silverstep]].

## Summary

The conclusion of the Battle of Silverstep, in four movements: [[Lord Darian]]'s arrogant "empty goblet" vow; a midnight council where [[Riven]] and [[Cassandra]] gamble the city on a trap at [[Willowshade]]; the battle itself, won by feint, ridge-archery, and storm magic; and **Cassandra's death** and lake funeral.

## Key Beats

**The Empty Goblet.** [[Lord Darian]] vows not to drink until it's Silverstep's wine in his cup, dismissing [[Cassandra]] as a thief and bandit. Two hundred Golden Talons dress ranks.

**Council in the Dark.** [[Riven]] arrives with thirty Rangers (having sent [[Ekundayo]] north to reinforce [[Serenko]]). With Cassandra and sergeants [[Halvik]] and [[Senn]], they reject suicide and street-fighting for a trap: bait Darian's host across the ford into the valley throat at [[Willowshade]].

**Willowshade.** Forty halberds feign collapse and retreat across the ford; Darian, mistaking it for a rout, drives his men into the valley. Rangers loose from both ridges — officers and drummers drop — and storm magic (hail, sleet, blizzard) shatters the Talons' discipline. **[[Riven]]'s flaming arrow** punches through Darian's breastplate; the Talons break.

**The price.** A spear bursts through [[Cassandra]]'s ribs as she steadies the line. [[Riven]]'s healing fails against the wound; her last words: *"Then… don't waste it."* That night her body is burned on a boat across the lake, the city silent on the shore.
