# RP 14.1 — Pavel Shoots his Shot

> **Source**: RP14.1 (companion scene to the Great Feast)
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Pavel]] (POV), [[Astor]]
> **Setting**: Later in the Great Feast at [[Nightmarch]], as the young people clear a dance floor.

## Summary

Watching [[Edina Aldori]] perform **[[The Sword and the Suitor]]** dance — and choose [[Astor]] as her Suitor — [[Pavel]] spirals into drunken jealousy over the gulf between his kennel upbringing and their aristocratic world. He charges to fight Astor, but when Edina steps between them and says his name, he loses his nerve and vomits on her boots.

## Key Beats

**The dance.** Edina dances the Sword and the Suitor using a novelty **"Pavel Stick"** as the Sword, and points it at [[Astor]] to choose him. [[Linzi]] narrates the courtly form to a drinking Pavel. Pavel realizes Astor *anticipates* the Sword's strikes by reading Edina's pattern — and that Astor and Edina come from the same privileged world he never could.

**The break.** Bile rising, Pavel resolves to beat Astor and somehow "win" Edina. He stalks the floor snarling; outside, [[Cat]] gives a warning roar.

**The collapse.** Edina steps between them: *"Pavel? Are you okay?"* The shock that **she remembers his name** breaks the dam — and instead of words, he throws up all over her boots, then tries to disappear.

## Characters Present / Mentioned

- [[Pavel]] — drunk, heartsick, humiliated
- [[Edina Aldori]] — dances and chooses Astor; remembers Pavel from [[Restov]]
- [[Astor]] — her chosen Suitor; nearly fought
- [[Linzi]] — narrates the dance to Pavel
- [[Cat]] — warns from outside

- Pivotal beat for [[Pavel and Edina]]: confirms their shared history in [[Restov]] (before her sister [[Jolia Aldori]]'s death) and that **she remembers him**.
- Ties to [[The Sword and the Suitor]] (the courtly dance) and the running "Pavel Stick" gag.
