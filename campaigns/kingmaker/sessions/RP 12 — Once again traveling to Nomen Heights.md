# RP 12 — Once again traveling to Nomen Heights

> **Source**: RP12 (camp with the rescued Varnholders; Astor plays [[Benedin]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Setting**: A campfire on the [[Nomen Heights]], escorting the rescued people of [[Varnhold]] home, [[Vordakai]]'s severed head in a bag.

## Summary

Alive and victorious after the tomb, [[Riven]] dares to hope again and asks the group what they hope for next. The answers map the party's near future — and [[Benedin]] reveals the darker tools and threats now in play: the [[Eclipsed Incantations]], the [[The Arsonist of Bronzeshield Hold|arsonist]], and a ritual that can imprison a being forever.

## Key Beats

**The eye-gem's pull.** [[Vordakai's Eye-Gem]], wrapped in cloth in the satchel, tugs at everyone's mind — all keep wanting a closer look.

**Pavel's hopes.** [[Pavel]] (nursing the incapacitated [[Lucas]] with broth from a mechanical udder [[Milo]] built) wants Lucas back on his feet, time with the animals, and to check on [[Bird]], who isn't thriving. Riven jokes about [[Brumble]] and mentions [[Astor]]'s newly built mansion and a grove being built in [[Nightmarch]].

**Benedin's burdens.** [[Benedin]] reports some young kin have returned to [[Bronzeshield Hold]] thanks to [[Linzi]]'s words, but he's preoccupied with revenge on a *"red-haired devil"* — the [[The Arsonist of Bronzeshield Hold|arsonist]] he can't seem to catch. He's studying the **[[Eclipsed Incantations]]**: a binding-circle ritual that might explain the night demons, and — more chilling — *"a way to trap someone forever,"* for things that cannot die.

**Pavel on imprisonment.** Pavel calls eternal imprisonment *"a fate worse than death,"* and dismisses any god wanting him; he'd rather rest until reborn.
