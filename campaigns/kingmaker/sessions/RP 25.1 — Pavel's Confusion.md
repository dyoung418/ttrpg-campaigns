# RP 25.1 — Pavel's Confusion

> **Source**: RP25.1 (companion scene to RP25, the [[Pavel and Edina]] courtyard confession — Pavel processing [[Edina Aldori|Edina]]'s words with [[Astor]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Pavel]], [[Astor]] (also mentioned: [[Riven]], [[Milo]], [[Lee]], [[Lucas]])
> **Setting**: The hills above [[Oaktop]], a moonless night, on the eve of an ambush against [[Nyrissa]]'s forces looting the silver mine.

## Summary

On a hilltop overlooking the ruined village of [[Oaktop]] — burned to ash by [[Nyrissa]]'s forces, its silver mine now worked by redcaps, trolls, and satyrs under lantern-light — [[Pavel]] sits and broods about women instead of the coming battle. He has already silently killed the hilltop guards, leaving the valley blind. As [[Astor]] clanks up to coordinate the ambush ([[Riven]]'s forces will feint from the west to draw the enemy out), Pavel can't stop replaying his morning conversation with [[Edina Aldori|Edina]] (the RP25 courtyard confession — see [[Pavel and Edina]]).

So he asks Astor — a married man — what it means when a woman says she wants to be *friends*. Astor brews travel-sized tea while the battle kicks off around them and tries to answer.

## Key Beats

**The question.** *"Astor, you understand women, right?"* Pavel wants to know what it means when a woman says she wants to be friends. Astor, distracted and pouring tea badly, admits *"I told my now-wife I wanted to be friends, because I was scared it might be too much to ask for more."* Then he turns it back on Pavel: what would it mean if *Pavel* said it?

**Pavel's blind spot.** Pavel thinks hard. His only "friends" are the people he kills things with — and [[Linzi]]. *"Is that what women do with their friends? Go kill things? That doesn't sound right…"* He's genuinely lost.

**Astor's wisdom (mid-battle).** As the ambush begins — [[Lucas]]'s illusory fireball-and-feint scattering the trolls, arrows raining from the dark — Astor curses (the tea set was a gift from his in-laws; his wife will kill him if he loses it), grabs his horned helm, and delivers the line plainly: *"Friends aren't the people you take lives with. They're the people you share life with!"* He calls back to the rest of the party hidden behind them: *"Remember them in the moments to come!"*

**The charge.** A robed troll shaman raises a skull-topped staff and looses a fireball into the grouped archers — half vanish in illusory mist (Pavel chuckles that [[Lucas]] will brag for a week), the rest "flee" to bait the trolls and satyrs into a charge. Pavel leaps from his rock, transforming one of Astor's tea cups into "travel-sized shards" as he goes. *"The shaman is mine."* [[Lee]] sprints past laughing; [[Rindle (Construct)|Rindle]]'s engines whoosh behind. As they charge downhill together, Pavel keeps probing — *"Theoretically, if a woman said she respected you and didn't want to play games, and she liked Cat and wanted to be your friend, what would you do next? Theoretically."*
