# RP 26 — Riven's Journey to Kyonin

> **Source**: RP26 (a long six-part vignette; written by Wes/Riven — *"essential in Riven's character arc"*)
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Riven]] (with [[Linzi]]; this is the account of his three-month diplomatic absence)
> **Setting**: The road south from [[Freehaven]], through the [[Embeth Forest]], into [[Kyonin]] and [[Lyriel]]'s garden.

## Summary

The full account of [[Riven]]'s three-month absence: he leads Freehaven's delegation to [[Kyonin]], negotiates recognition and the [[Freehaven-Kyonin Treaty]], and — at last — goes to [[Lyriel]] to tell the truth about why he left twenty years ago. With [[Linzi]] keeping him honest the whole way, the reunion ends not in reconciliation-as-reward but in a fragile, real *"again."*

## Key Beats

**1 — The Road South.** Riven runs the diplomatic column like a ranger campaign (to courtiers' dismay), reading terrain and weather. [[Linzi]] needles him about constantly looking south, naming what he won't: this trip is about more than trade.

**2 — The Embeth Forest.** [[Jameel Visser]]'s foresters meet them; the old friends clasp arms. Over maps they reach the informal **Embeth Agreement** (safe conduct, shared warnings). Jameel's verdict: *"Now you look like you know what you're for. Keep her close, Riven — she makes you almost approachable."*

**3 — At the Border of Kyonin.** Crossing into his homeland near [[Avennara]], Riven is shaken by memory. Linzi presses him until he admits aloud: *"I mean to find [[Lyriel]]."* Her counsel: go as himself, not as a king presenting proof — *"you cannot arrive with a crown and a record of victories and expect her heart to salute."*

**4 — The Amulet.** Alone at night, Riven holds [[Lyriels Amulet|Lyriel's amulet]] (moon and rose) and finally names the real reason he left: not honor, but shame — *"I could not bear for you to see me ruined and loving me still."* He resolves to return to her *honestly*, not remade.

**5 — Reunion in the Garden.** At [[Lyriel]]'s house (found via [[Thaelirian]]'s directions), Riven confesses everything: that he loved her, left her, was wrong, mistook self-punishment for honor. Lyriel reveals she **received the letter** but didn't come to the tournament because she didn't know whether he'd written to *her* or to "a memory wearing my name." She says *"I would have stayed."* No reconciliation — but: *"I would like to speak again. Not today. But again."*

**6 — Endings and New Beginnings.** The **[[Freehaven-Kyonin Treaty]]** is signed (recognition, trade, Kyonin scholars/wardens to study the elven ruins in Freehaven with Freehaven escort). Riven returns to Lyriel's garden; when he answers her like a council report, she stops him: *"I did not ask for a report. Tell me what it is like."*
