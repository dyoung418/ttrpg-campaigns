# RP 11 — Rest and Relaxation at Silverstep

> **Source**: RP11 (lakeside downtime; Danny voices [[Linzi]]; Astor plays [[Benedin]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Setting**: The lakeshore at [[Silverstep]], the most idyllic town in [[Freehaven]].

## Summary

Resting at [[Silverstep]], [[Riven]] is moved by how much the lakeside town resembles [[Avennara]], where he grew up — and finally opens up about **[[Lyriel]]**, the love he left behind in [[Kyonin]]. With [[Linzi]]'s encouragement, he resolves to reach out to her. Meanwhile [[Benedin]] frets over drawing his scattered kin back to [[Bronzeshield Hold]].

## Key Beats

**Avennara and Lyriel.** Silverstep reminds Riven of his birthplace, [[Avennara]]. He says [[Lyriel]] would love it here — she'd set up her easel and paint. He shows the amulet she gave him at their parting: a moon and a rose. He calls leaving her *"a foolish mistake"* and his deepest regret, and asks [[Linzi]] whether he should write her a letter or return to [[Kyonin]]. Linzi (a writer) eagerly offers to help. **This is the seed of [[Lyriel's Wait]] and [[Riven's Letter to Lyriel]].**

**Linzi and the Gazette.** Linzi confesses she's deliberately *not* written up Silverstep in the *Freehaven Gazette* for fear of overrunning it — but she's nearly done with a piece on [[Bronzeshield Hold]].

**Bronzeshield's slow return.** Asked how the hold fares, [[Benedin]] reports precious few of his kin have returned — mostly builders and merchants — and pins hope on Linzi's article to reach his scattered family. Riven promises to spread word to [[Mivon]] and [[Restov]], with whom Freehaven now has relations.
