# RP 14 — The Great Feast of Freehaven

> **Source**: RP14 (the centerpiece feast; Danny voices [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Riven]], [[Pavel]], [[Astor]], [[Lucas]], [[Lee]], [[Milo]]
> **Setting**: The grand hall of [[Nightmarch]]'s Old Fortress, celebrating victory over [[Vordakai]] and the restoration of [[Varnhold]].

## Summary

The great feast of [[Freehaven]] — banners of the seven towns and the [[Nomen Centaurs]], leaders and peoples from across the kingdom. [[Riven]] gives the unity speech, secretly hosts a glamoured [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]], entrusts [[Astor]] with his affairs should he fall, courts a Restov alliance, offers [[Octavia]] the mayorship of [[Varnhold]], and closes by announcing the **Grand Tournament**.

## Key Beats

**Riven's speech & the hooded guest.** Riven toasts Freehaven and "allies old and new," nodding to a hooded figure — the disguised [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]], whose identity [[Pavel]] is assigned to guard.

**Riven's testament to Astor.** Privately, Riven tells [[Astor]] he's learned **[[Nyrissa]]** ("Narissa") is behind everything — [[The Stag Lord]], [[Hargulka]], the Owlbear, the [[Cult of the Bloom]], even [[Vordakai]] — and he must see it through. He gives Astor a sealed letter for **Lyriel Anarindë** in [[Kyonin]] (see [[Riven's Letter to Lyriel]]) and names him successor should he not return. Astor, citing his family motto *"Endurance overcomes All,"* vows to keep the letter and return it on his *own* deathbed.

**Octavia & Varnhold.** Riven offers [[Octavia]] the mayorship of [[Varnhold]]; she asks for time to think.

**Jamandi's reckoning.** In her POV, [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]] marvels that the "motley six" she'd written off — she'd bet on [[Maegar Varn]] — have thrived in war, diplomacy, and commerce. She resolves to draw closer to Freehaven, worried about [[King Irovetti]] and "Nyrissa." She reveals Irovetti's secret: born illegitimate as **Mandalarucio**, orphaned young, tangled with the **Technic League**, and risen via mysterious powerful devices.

**The Tournament announced.** Riven announces a team **Grand Tournament** three months out — five events (battle of bards, drinking/eating, labyrinth escape, monster hunt, archery). See [[Grand Tournament of Freehaven]].
