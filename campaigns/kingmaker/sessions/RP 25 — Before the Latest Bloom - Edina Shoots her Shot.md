# RP 25 — Before the Latest Bloom - Edina Shoots her Shot

> **Source**: RP25 (the courtyard confession; Danny voices [[Edina Aldori]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Pavel]] (POV), with [[Edina Aldori]] (GM)
> **Setting**: The Old Fortress courtyard at [[Nightmarch]], at dawn, as the party prepares to march against [[Nyrissa]]'s bloom forces (the [[Oaktop]] operation).

## Summary

As [[Pavel]] checks his pack before marching out, [[Edina Aldori]] crosses the courtyard to find him deliberately. She apologizes for the feast, tells him plainly that she thinks about him *"in a way that is not ordinary,"* and — through Pavel's suspicion and pain — asks to be his friend. He accepts; she helps him into his pack and tells him to be careful.

## Key Beats

**The approach.** Edina comes straight at Pavel (knowing he'd see through anything else), apologizes for the feast, and notes wryly the boots he ruined were old ones she'd meant to replace. Pavel, changed — meeting her eyes, even smiling — promises her new boots are safe: *"I haven't had anything to drink."*

**Gratitude for Jolia.** She thanks him for the hunting competition (Session 30) where the party fought to save her and her sister [[Jolia Aldori]] — *"you knew that"* cost them points. *"I have never forgotten whose face I saw when I looked up from the ground."*

**The confession.** Citing [[The Sword and the Suitor]] (Jolia's favorite, but *"not a good model for a real relationship — I don't want to keep someone guessing if I care about them"*), she says plainly: *"I think about you, Pavel. More than I have any ordinary reason to."*

**Pavel's wall, then yield.** Pavel suspects a game or [[Astor]]'s prank, voice raw with old pain; [[Cat]] steps protectively between them. When she insists she'd never toy with him, he reddens — *"Oh. Oh."* — and asks, almost inaudibly, *"Are you saying you'd like to be … friends?"* She says yes, helps him into his pack as no one has since he was a boy, and tells him: *"Be careful."*
