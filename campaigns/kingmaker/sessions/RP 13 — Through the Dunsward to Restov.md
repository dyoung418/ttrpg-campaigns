# RP 13 — Through the Dunsward to Restov

> **Source**: RP13 (a long, comedic ancestor-prank scene on the road)
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Pavel]], [[Lucas]]
> **Setting**: A campfire on the Dunsward steppe, en route to [[Restov]].

## Summary

[[Pavel]] corners [[Lucas]] at the fire to demand he show more respect to [[Great Aunt Sumi]] — then escalates the bit into an elaborate, straight-faced prank that he intends to *marry* Sumi, complete with a spectral fox wedding. The gag spirals until [[Cat]] intervenes and the whole "vision" is revealed as a haunting by [[Great Uncle Olaf]].

## Key Beats

**The respect lecture.** Pavel relays Sumi's grievances: Lucas slurs *"greataunt"* as one word, only communes when he needs a remedy (he once forgot her birthday entirely), and treats her like a medical reference instead of the renowned cook and dating-advisor she was. He insists on *"O Great Aunt Sumi."*

**The fake wedding.** Pavel "reveals" Sumi appearing beside him in a wedding dress; a spectral ferret nuzzles [[Cat]] with four kits underfoot. He announces he'll be Lucas's "future Great Uncle" and demands wedding presents.

**Lucas fights back.** Suspecting possession, Lucas casts *Sending* into Pavel's mind — and gets a terrified reply: *"That BEAST isn't supposed to be here!"* [[Cat]] swells and ROARS; "Pavel" shreds away to reveal **[[Great Uncle Olaf]]**, who had been impersonating Pavel, with Sumi beating him about the head. Lucas wakes — it was a dream/haunting; the real Pavel is on the ridge sending [[Bird]] to hunt.

## Characters Present / Mentioned

- [[Pavel]] — straight-faced prankster (and the form Olaf wore)
- [[Lucas]] — defends himself against the "possession"; casts Sending
- [[Great Aunt Sumi]] — the aggrieved ancestor at the center of it
- [[Great Uncle Olaf]] — revealed as the trickster behind "Pavel"
- [[Cat]] — breaks the illusion with a world-shaking roar
- [[Bird]] — the real Pavel's companion at dawn

## Locations

- The Dunsward steppe, on the road to [[Restov]]
