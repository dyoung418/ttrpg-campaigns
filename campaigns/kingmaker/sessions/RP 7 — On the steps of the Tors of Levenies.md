# RP 7 — On the steps of the Tors of Levenies

> **Source**: RP7 (camp on the stairs; Astor plays [[Benedin]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Riven]], [[Pavel]], [[Lucas]], [[Astor]] (as [[Benedin]]), [[Lee]], [[Milo]]
> **Setting**: A narrow landing on the jagged stone steps climbing into the misted [[Tors of Levenies]], on the way to confront [[Vordakai]].

## Summary

Camped on the cursed stairs, the party debates whether they're ready for what waits above. [[Lucas]] voices doubts about their fitness to lead and the lack of an "end goal"; [[Riven]] reframes their role as the shield and sword of [[Freehaven]]; [[Pavel]] insists his only plan is to kill dead things tomorrow.

## Key Beats

**Riven's resolve.** The place reminds Riven of the cursed corners of [[Kyonin]] where he spent fifty years fighting [[Treerazer]]'s undead. He swears they won't let corruption take root here.

**Lucas's doubts.** [[Lucas]] admits they've often survived on luck, not legend, and asks what they're even doing — are they on a quest for Riven to atone? He names their real burden: they are the *leaders of Freehaven*; their actions are Acts of State, and the enemies they make become Freehaven's enemies. He counsels they must not ally with evil or drag the people into wars that aren't theirs.

**Pavel the sentinel.** Pavel rejects "end goals": *"I'm going to kill some dead things, or I'm going to die."* He tallies the growing "pack" — the six of them, plus [[Cat]], [[Rindle (Construct)|Rindle]], [[Bird]], Astor, [[Brumble]] (*"a little chubby to be a defender these days"*).

**Riven names the enemy.** Varnhold, the Nomen, and their research all point to **[[Vordakai]]**, an ancient undead necromancer. Riven also admits the great regret of his life: he *"let myself get distracted by love just once"* — a first hint of [[Lyriel]].
