# RP 1 — Camping with Ekundayo

> **Source**: RP1 (early-campaign bonding scene)
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Riven]], [[Pavel]], [[Astor]]
> **Setting**: A weeklong scouting trip with [[Ekundayo]] through the northern reaches of the Greenbelt.

## Summary

On a long scouting expedition with the enigmatic ranger [[Ekundayo]], the party tries to bond. [[Pavel]], unable to read humanoids, gives up on impressing Ekundayo and turns to his dog, **Dog** — and while scratching the dog's ears, unexpectedly opens up. Around the same fires, [[Riven]] shares a long-held shame from his past.

## Key Beats

**Pavel's kennel childhood.** Scratching Dog's ears, Pavel quietly mentions working in a kennel from a young age after his mother died, sleeping in a room beside the kennel, and remembering those dogs fondly. (Groundwork for [[Pavel's Parentage]].)

**Riven's disgrace.** After five days in the wild, Riven confides a major failure: he left his life as a captain in the elven army because a patrol he led missed an approaching army that massacred several elven villages — his patrol had tarried too long in a nearby village at his leadership. He fought valiantly on their return but was disgraced and left. He still carries deep shame, and does **not** say why he tarried in the village.

## Characters Present / Mentioned

- [[Riven]] — confesses the failure that ended his elven army career
- [[Pavel]] — opens up about his kennel upbringing
- [[Astor]] — present on the trip
- [[Ekundayo]] — the ranger the party is bonding with; owns the dog "Dog"

## Locations

- Northern reaches of the Greenbelt (the Stolen Lands wilds around [[Nightmarch]])

## Notable Details / Flags

- Riven's unspoken reason for tarrying in the village is a deliberate hook — left unresolved here.
