# RP 3 — After the Stag Lord Fight

> **Source**: RP3 (the morning after the Stag Lord's defeat)
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Pavel]], [[Lucas]]
> **Setting**: Dawn at [[The Stag Lord's Fort]], the day after the battle.

## Summary

The morning after [[The Stag Lord]] falls, [[Pavel]] interrupts [[Lucas]]'s meditation to ask about something he saw while [[Lucas]]'s healing magic pulled him back from the brink: an old white-haired woman with pointed ears and orange **fox eyes** who glared at him before dissolving into Lucas's face.

## Key Beats

**The vision.** As the Stag Lord's arrow dropped him, Pavel heard his dead mother's voice and went cold like a river (Lucas's healing) — and saw a disapproving fox-eyed crone. Lucas identifies her as **[[Great Aunt Sumi]]**, a powerful healer-ancestor with a famously poor bedside manner, and notes that the ancestors feel unusually *close* in this place.

**Pavel's request.** Pavel, who never knew his father, asks to meet his father's family — but warns Lucas, with predatory intensity, that he will not be made a fool by theatrics. He stalks off; [[Cat]] (visibly growing larger by the day) lingers to stare Lucas down.

**Lucas's advice.** Lucas calls after him that for real answers about family, *"journals and going back to one's home"* serve better than oracles and sages.

## Characters Present / Mentioned

- [[Lucas]] — names the ancestor; offers a glimpse of Pavel's own ancestors

- [[Cat]] — growing steadily; stares down Lucas on Pavel's behalf
