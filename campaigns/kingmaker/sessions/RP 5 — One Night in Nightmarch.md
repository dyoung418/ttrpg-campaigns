# RP 5 — One Night in Nightmarch

> **Source**: RP5 (a winter evening at the inn; after Hargulka's fortress, before the trip to Restov)
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Riven]], [[Pavel]]
> **Setting**: The common room of the [[Rise n Dine]] in [[Nightmarch]], a cold winter night.

## Summary

[[Pavel]], deciding [[Riven]] needs rescuing from three giggling young women, scowls them off and joins him for a drink. What follows is one of their deepest conversations — Riven on the weight of ruling, Pavel on leaders and followers via the animal kingdom.

## Key Beats

**"Can we do better?"** Riven confesses ruling is nothing like he imagined: he spent his life fighting tyrants and despots and wants *a different ending* for this land. He asks Pavel whether they can do better.

**Pavel's bestiary of leadership.** Pavel answers obliquely — lion prides, wolf alphas, herds led by an old female — concluding there are many kinds of leaders and followers, and *"maybe the trick is to match 'em up right so it works."* Riven calls him *"the wisest of all of us."*

**Nyrissa's shadow.** Riven voices his dread: the fey **Narissa** ([[Nyrissa]]) seems to have influence wherever power is abused; the incidents aren't isolated. She *came to him* — and he fears it's because he could be corrupted like [[The Stag Lord]] or [[Hargulka]].

**Pavel's read on Riven.** Half-drunk, Pavel tells Riven he's no Stag Lord and no Hargulka — *"an elf who cares. Probably cares too much."* He guesses Riven left his homeland voluntarily over his old failure and can't let it go: *"a part of you is still in that village... but the past only exists in our minds. We're not those people anymore."*
