# RP 9 — In the Sepulchar Chamber

> **Source**: RP9 (dungeon investigation; Astor plays [[Benedin]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Setting**: A ritual sepulchre deep in [[Vordakai's Island]], under the gaze of stone skeletal watchers.

## Summary

Searching for the captive [[Xamanthe]] and the missing Varnholders, the party works through a sinister ritual chamber — altars, skeletal statues, and blood-residue lamps hinting at sacrifices to **Charon**. They resolve not to meddle with the dark vessels, then throw open the far door into whatever waits beyond.

## Key Beats

**The trail.** [[Riven]] reads a faint hoofprint and leads the group up into the ritual room, dread climbing his spine: *"What terrible deeds were wrought here? Why do they have Xamanthe?"*

**The blood lamps.** [[Pavel]] spots empty lamps by the statues with a residue of blood. [[Benedin]] explains that rituals may need only a drop of blood, but warns that empowering **Charon** or his agents endangers others elsewhere. Pavel theorizes the lamps forge a connection between Charon and the blood's owner — perhaps enough to turn an ordinary Varnholder mage into a flying undead horror.

**The door.** Agreeing to leave the unholy vessels alone, Riven and Pavel listen at the exit. Riven hears noises beyond; the party lines up, weapons and spells ready — *"Vordekai's never seen anything like us"* — and Pavel throws open the door. **BOOM.** *Oops.*
