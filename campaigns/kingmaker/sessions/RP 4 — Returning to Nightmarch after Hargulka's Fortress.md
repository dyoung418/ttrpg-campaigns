# RP 4 — Returning to Nightmarch after Hargulka's Fortress

> **Source**: RP4 (campfire scene on the march home)
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Pavel]], [[Astor]]
> **Setting**: The last campfire before the party returns to [[Nightmarch]] after the campaign against [[Hargulka]]'s trolls.

## Summary

On the final night before returning from the troll campaign, [[Pavel]] surprises everyone by sitting beside [[Astor]] and asking about the story of how he got his horns. Astor explains his family's fairy-godmother tradition — and the curse-blessing that gave him his stag's horns.

## Key Beats

**Astor's horns.** Noble houses keep fairy godmothers; at births the fey offer blessings. Astor's family's usual godmother was sick when he was born, so a hex-prone witch came instead and spun "the nicest curse she could" — the stag horns that erupt when he's angered. Astor admits the beast pulls at him more and more, and he fears one day he won't turn back.

**Fey on the land.** Astor links this to ruling: hold land with fey on it and you must find where you stand with them — citing [[Riven]] befriending the river fey ([[Melianse]], who troubled the loggers). He's unsure whether the green-hair-shedding [[Nyrissa|fairy queen]] is the same fey, noting *"sometimes fey change their faces."*

**Pavel's wish.** Pavel asks, half-embarrassed, whether a fairy godmother could turn *him* into an animal. He explains animals are *genuine* — Cat hisses when angry, purrs when happy, *"would never claim to be your friend and then make fun of you"* — but he doesn't want to stop being himself, for his mother's sake. [[Cat]] leans into him, staring down Astor.
