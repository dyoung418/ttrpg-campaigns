# RP 19 — The Forest Answers

> **Source**: RP19 (Ranger ambush vignette; Danny voices [[Almon d'Avoine]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Riven]] (with his Rangers; [[Tovan]])
> **Setting**: A forest road during the Pitax War, ambushing an [[King Irovetti|Irovetti]] supply column bound for [[Silverstep]].

## Summary

[[Riven]] and his Rangers annihilate a Pitax supply column in a silent forest ambush — mercy for the broken, arrows for the defiant. From a terrified driver he learns the **[[Golden Talons]]** (two battalions) march on [[Silverstep]] and [[Cassandra]]. Unknown to them, the Pitax operative [[Almon d'Avoine]] survives the slaughter by faking his own death.

## Key Beats

**The ambush.** Wagons stamped with Irovetti's golden goblet burn as Rangers cut down the escort. [[Riven]] moves through it without a wasted word, his [[Feywind]] bow trailing fire and astral light.

**The intelligence.** A defiant captain spits threats; a trembling driver breaks: *"The Golden Talons. Two battalions. They march. For Silverstep."* Riven sends prisoners east to the centaurs, spares the broken — and turns toward Silverstep at once: the Talons outnumber Cassandra two to one.

**Almon survives.** [[Almon d'Avoine]] — sensing the ambush by the *silence of the birds* — hides beneath the wagon, then stages a fatal "arrow through the eye" with a dead guard's helm and lies still. He overhears everything, fixes on his new target — **[[Cassandra]]** — and slips away to scheme.
