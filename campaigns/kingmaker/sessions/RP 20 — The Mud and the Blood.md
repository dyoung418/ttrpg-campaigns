# RP 20 — The Mud and the Blood

> **Source**: RP20 (battle vignette from [[Cassandra]]'s perspective; written by Wes/Riven)
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: None (GM/author vignette — POV [[Cassandra]])
> **Setting**: The meadows outside [[Silverstep]], the first clash against the Pitax vanguard.

## Summary

[[Cassandra]] and twenty of the [[Silverstep]] Guard meet the Pitax vanguard — sixty mercenaries under [[Lord Darian]] — at a hedgerow and ditch. Her drilled halberdiers bloody the enemy but cannot hold against the weight. She falls back in good order, leaving three dead, as Darian contemptuously declines to chase.

## Key Beats

**The stand at the ditch.** Cassandra's Guard — [[Halvik]] roaring cadence, and young Joren and Luko among them — brace and trade blows across the ditch, dragging mercenaries down with halberd-hooks.

**Numbers tell.** Sixty against twenty: the line bends. Cassandra orders a fighting retreat by files; three of her men lie dead, five wounded. [[Lord Darian]] reins in, content to let them bleed — Silverstep already his in his mind — knowing his main force arrives in days.

**Cassandra's resolve.** She reflects on her past under [[The Stag Lord]] and the trust [[Freehaven]] gave her; she'll stand to the last, but reads the truth in the numbers: they cannot stop Pitax's weight alone.
