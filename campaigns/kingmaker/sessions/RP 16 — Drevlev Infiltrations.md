# RP 16 — Drevlev Infiltrations

> **Source**: RP16 (infiltration planning + cellar scene; Astor plays [[Benedin]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Players present**: [[Astor]] (as [[Benedin]]), [[Lucas]], [[Pavel]], [[Riven]], [[Lee]], [[Milo]]
> **Setting**: Inside [[Fort Drevlev]] during the infiltration — the guard rotation, the resistance, and the keep's cellar prison.

## Summary

[[Benedin]], having enlisted in the Drevlev guard, forges the duty roster to assign himself to guard the high-value hostage [[Tamary Numesti]], then connects with the resistance and the imprisoned [[Terrion Numesti|Lord Numesti]]. The party coordinates reconnaissance of [[Fort Drevlev|the keep]] via [[Lucas]]'s *Sending* and [[Pavel]]'s stealth.

## Key Beats

**Forged orders.** Benedin alters the shift roster (a forgery check) to put himself over [[Tamary Numesti]] rather than [[Terrion Numesti|Lord Numesti]] — less suspicious — and passes Messages to [[Satinder Morne]] and the resistance.

**Recon plan.** The group plans: [[Pavel]] (effectively undetectable) will hunt for secret entrances on the hillside and treeline, climb the tower to map the windows, while [[Lucas]] keeps contact with Benedin via *Sending*. Open questions: other resistance members, the patrolling **hill giants**, and what might be hidden at the temple.

**The cellar.** Alone with [[Terrion Numesti|Lord Numesti]], Benedin learns why he didn't flee — *"My daughter was at stake"* — and that [[Hannis Drevlev]] (*"the most selfish man alive"*) surely has a secret passage he's told no one, not even his wife. Worse: [[Terrion Numesti|Numesti]]'s captive daughter and the other prisoners [[King Irovetti]] demanded to appease **[[Armag Reborn]]** have already been **taken by Armag**. Terrion fears the worst.
