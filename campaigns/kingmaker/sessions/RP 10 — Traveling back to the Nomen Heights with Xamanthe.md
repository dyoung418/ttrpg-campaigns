# RP 10 — Traveling back to the Nomen Heights with Xamanthe

> **Source**: RP10 (morning camp scene; Danny voices [[Xamanthe]])
> **Status**: `rp-scene`
> **Setting**: Dawn on the [[Nomen Heights]], escorting the rescued [[Xamanthe]] home after [[Vordakai]]'s defeat.

## Summary

A bright, easy morning after the tomb. [[Riven]] brews tea for [[Xamanthe]] and teases her about a centaur-vs-two-legger race, while she — her leg curse newly lifted — begins to relax and pledges to repay the party's kindness, even as she worries for her herd's survival.

## Key Beats

**Riven's easy charm.** Gone is the gloom of the Tors; Riven pours tea and proposes a race, betting on [[Pavel]] or [[Hyram]].

**Xamanthe's gratitude.** Her legs are regaining movement after the Nomen enchanter removed [[Vordakai]]'s curse. She admits humans have not been kind to her people, but vows never to forget the party's sacrifice and to repay her debt — joking they can join the *kinderfoal* race at the spring festival. She frets: Vordakai was worse than legend, and she fears for the herd.

**Riven looks ahead.** He gently probes what she learned in Vordakai's tower and floats the idea of the Nomen herd backing a mission to ensure Vordakai is gone for good.

**Pavel & Cat comedy.** Pavel muses that [[Riven]]'s sunny mood is a "psychological regression to happier times." [[Cat]] fusses over invisible air beside [[Lucas]] (one of his ancestors), then snubs the dried meat Lucas offers — to Lucas's exasperation. Pavel worries again that [[Bird]] isn't thriving.
