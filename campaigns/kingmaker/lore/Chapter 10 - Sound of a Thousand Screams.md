# Chapter 10: Sound of a Thousand Screams

Chapter 10 is the climax of the Kingmaker Adventure Path, structured in three parts. PCs should be **level 17** entering Part 1, **level 18** before Part 2, and **level 19** before Part 3.

## Structure

- **[[The Month of Destruction|Part 1]]** — A Month of Destruction (level 17). [[Nyrissa]] unleashes 8 bloom events over 30 days against the PCs' kingdom.
- **Part 2** — A Thousand Breaths (level 18). The PCs enter [[Thousandbreaths]], Nyrissa's demiplane, and clear its nine glades.
- **Part 3** — House at the Edge of Time (level 19). The PCs storm [[House at the Edge of Time|Nyrissa's mansion]] and face her directly.

## The Calm Before

Chapter 10 should not begin until:
- The kingdom spans all four regions of the Stolen Lands
- Any lingering loose ends (Candlemere, wilderness encounters) are wrapped up
- PCs have ideally recovered [[Briar]] from Irovetti's palace

## Key Setup Hooks

**[[Evindra]]'s Warning** — [[Evindra]] (nereid; fey ally) warns the PCs that First World forces will assault the Stolen Lands. She explains [[Briar]], [[Nyrissa]]'s background and fall from grace, and points toward [[Thousandbreaths]] in the Thousand Voices forest. The PCs befriended her and gained her trust before she shared this intelligence.

**The Houten Legacy** (30 XP) — Aristocrat Belinda Houten will give the PCs a copy of *Zuddiger's Picnic* (a children's book that maps Thousandbreaths) if they build a Theater and four Monuments in her honor. **Critical item for navigating Part 2.**

**A Heroic Challenge** (30 XP) — Aurex Thewsen dares the PCs to slay the black dragon Ilthuliak. Reward: +1 max Fame/Infamy, monument from her remains.

## Briar Awakening

When recovered from Irovetti's palace, Briar is a *+3 greater striking cold iron bastard sword*. It gains Sharpness Points throughout Chapter 10 (see Appendix 4, page 585). It glows green and briefly reaches full power when the lesser jabberwock appears. Briar entering Thousandbreaths adds 1 Sharpness Point and alerts Nyrissa. Full Sharpness: vorpal, intelligent.

## Chapter Outcome Options

- **Nyrissa slain** — Thousandbreaths uproots and erodes to become the Castle of Knives. Her body rises on a pyre of black lightning. The Lantern King becomes a new threat (Chapter 11).
- **Nyrissa forgiven** — Possible via Forgive Foe action after defeating or cornering her; see Chapter 11 for the redemption path.
- **Nyrissa absorbs the Stolen Lands** — If blooms are not all defeated by Day 30 and Nyrissa succeeds on a flat check. Catastrophic outcome (see page 477).
