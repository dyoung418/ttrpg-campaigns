# The Founding of Freehaven

The kingdom of [[Freehaven]] was not built in a day — its history spans years of hard-won expansion, diplomacy, and conflict across the Stolen Lands.

## The Charter

The PCs were invited by [[Lady Jamandi Aldori]] of Brevoy to settle the lawless Stolen Lands, a region long plagued by bandits and monster activity. She issued them a charter giving them the right to establish a barony. At the same time, she issued a similar charter to [[Hannis Drevlev]], who would later turn against the kingdom.

## Defeating the Stag Lord

Before their barony could be established, the PCs had to eliminate the [[The Stag Lord|Stag Lord]], a notorious bandit lord who had made the Stolen Lands his domain. His defeat cleared the way for the founding of [[Nightmarch]] and the formation of the barony.

## Growth and Expansion

Over years of in-game time, the kingdom grew from a small barony to a recognized kingdom spanning the full Stolen Lands. Key milestones:

- **Leveton** — Grew from [[Lady Jamandi Aldori|Oleg's Trading Post]], one of the PCs' first stops in the region
- **Bronzeshield Hold** — The PCs cleared trolls from this ancient dwarven stronghold and invited the exiled dwarves to reclaim it. [[Benedin]] and his people joined Freehaven.
- **The Mystery of Varnhold** — [[Vordakai]], an ancient cyclops lich freshly awoken after centuries, descended on [[Varnhold]] (a town founded by fellow charter-holder [[Maegar Varn]]) and harvested every soul to fill in his knowledge of the modern world. The PCs solved the mystery, freed what souls they could, and defeated Vordakai — but Maegar Varn was already dead. The surviving townspeople joined Freehaven.
- **Ashenfel and the Nomen Centaurs** — Vordakai had also captured the daughter of the [[Nomen Centaurs|Nomen Centaur]] ruler. After her rescue, the Nomens pledged their loyalty and [[Ashenfel]] joined the kingdom.
- **The Fall of Fort Drevlev** — [[Hannis Drevlev]] conspired with [[Pitax]] and the Tiger Lords against Freehaven. The PCs defeated all three. [[Fort Drevlev]] was absorbed.
- **The Conquest of Pitax** — [[King Irovetti]], the vain tyrant of Pitax, was overthrown by the PCs. [[Pitax]] — the largest city they had yet absorbed — joined Freehaven. Unbeknownst to most, Irovetti had been a pawn of [[Nyrissa]], who engineered his madness to make Freehaven stronger.

## The Present Threat

Now at its height, the kingdom faces its greatest threat: [[Nyrissa]], a fey queen who has been deliberately cultivating Freehaven's growth for centuries. The kingdom is meant to be the **1,000th and final** grain of sand in her Apology. See [[Chapter 10 - Sound of a Thousand Screams]] and [[The Month of Destruction]] for current events.
