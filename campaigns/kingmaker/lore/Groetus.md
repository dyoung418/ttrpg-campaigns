# Groetus

> **Type**: Deity (Pathfinder god of the end times)
> **Followers in campaign**: [[Harrim]]

## Summary

The god of the end times — an entropic deity of inevitability and the death of all things. Harrim, [[Freehaven]]'s Viceroy, serves Groetus as a cleric. The faith expresses itself as a kind of resigned peace; Harrim is calm and level-headed, only occasionally interrupted by declarations like *"Abandon hope and embrace the inevitable."* He means it in a comforting way, more or less.
