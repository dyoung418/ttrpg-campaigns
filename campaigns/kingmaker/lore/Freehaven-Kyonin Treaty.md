# The Freehaven-Kyonin Treaty

Signed during [[Riven]]'s three-month diplomatic mission to [[Kyonin]]. Negotiated jointly by Riven (field matters: roads, patrols, borders, wild country) and [[Linzi]] (tariffs, recognition, embassies).

## Terms

- **Recognition**: Kyonin recognizes Freehaven as a sovereign neighbor.
- **Trade**: Trade routes open between the two realms.
- **Elven ruins access**: Kyonin sends wardens and scholars to the old elven ruins within Freehaven's borders.
- **Freehaven's obligations**: Freehaven provides guides, escorts, supplies, and protection for those expeditions.

## Process

Three days of negotiation, six clerks, two maps, and one extended disagreement over "reasonable access." When talk turned to terrain and patrol, Riven answered plainly. When it turned to tariffs and recognition, Linzi entered with such precision that one of Kyonin's captains began watching her the way men watch drawn steel.

Riven set his seal beside Kyonin's without flourish: ink, sand, signatures, witnesses. A clerk checked the margins before the wax cooled.

Linzi's summary on the road home: *"We secured recognition, trade, access to the ruins, and the possibility of a letter. Not a poor showing."*

## Significance

Freehaven entered Kyonin as a question and left as a kingdom.
